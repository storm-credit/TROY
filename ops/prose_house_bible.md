# Prose House Bible

## 1. Sentence Goal

설명보다 감각이 먼저 도착하게 쓴다.

핵심:
- setting terms stay in canon docs
- prose converts them into air, light, fabric, water, distance, or temperature

## 2. Metaphor Families

한 문단에는 되도록 하나의 이미지 축만 쓴다.

available families:
- air
- light
- water
- paper
- fabric
- distance
- temperature

avoid:
- one paragraph mixing too many image systems

## 3. Character-Specific Texture

### Seojun

- gray air
- closed rooms
- wired earphones
- distance measured in steps
- light arriving late

### Arin

- clear breath
- spring cloth movement
- thin sunlight
- paper and voice together
- warmth without fantasy glow

### Their shared scenes

- desks
- notebook margins
- corridor light
- benches
- windows and reflected faces

## 4. Forbidden Prose

do not overuse:
- fate
- forever
- destiny
- miracle
- direct theory talk
- repeated music jargon

do not write:
- exposition disguised as poetry
- melodramatic crying language in every key scene
- summary sentences that explain what the reader already felt

## 5. Opening Sentence Modes

allowed episode opening types:
- weather and air first
- object first
- body posture first
- space and distance first

recommended:
- start from something small and external
- let emotion bloom one beat later

## 6. Ending Sentence Modes

allowed episode ending types:
- residue
- delayed recognition
- directional shift
- tiny decision
- unresolved but honest quiet

avoid:
- slogans
- moral statements
- chapter-summary endings

## 7. Music To Prose Conversion

instead of:
- dissonance
- resonance
- sync
- signal

prefer:
- the room turned thinner
- the air pressed closer
- light broke on the desk edge
- his body remembered the direction first
- her voice stayed like warmth after a cup was lifted

## 8. Emotional Rule

- hearing is power
- asking is love
- believing without proof is maturity
