# E085 Song Brief - 작은 성숙

## Identity

- Episode: E085
- Song function: Ending reflection
- Emotional thesis: 이해는 돌아온 소리가 아니라, 재촉하지 않는 침묵에서 시작됐다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "이번엔 맞히지 않고, 조금 늦게 기다렸다."
- Seasonal air: folded clothes, returned book, closed piano lid, slow step
- Energy level: low, modestly steadier

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: dry chamber texture, low room air, slow footstep rhythm, unfinished but steadier motif
- Tempo feel: slow walking pulse
- Vocal distance: close and plain
- Arrangement: slightly steadier, never triumphant

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: folded clothes, returned book, closed lid, autumn field edge
- Point of view: small behavioral maturity
- Words to avoid: I am grown, healed, now I know, complete peace
- Do not overstate: The change is small and non-performative.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No triumphant growth cue and no complete-peace language.

## Delivery

The song should mark a tiny change in posture, not a completed transformation.

```text
Quiet Korean autumn reflection track, dry chamber texture, low room air, slow footstep rhythm, unfinished but steadier motif, close plain vocal. Mood: folded clothes, returned book, closed piano lid, slow walk near an autumn field edge, choosing to wait instead of guessing. Avoid healed language, now-I-know lyrics, triumphant growth cue, complete-peace framing, and famous song imitation.
```
