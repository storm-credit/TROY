# E103 Song Brief - 재회

## Identity

- Episode: E103
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 선택된 만남은 복원이 아니라, 이제는 말해야만 닿을 얼굴을 받아들이는 일이다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "잘 지냈어가 겹친 자리에서, 우리는 예전보다 더 말해야 닿을 얼굴이 됐다."
- Seasonal air: 짧은 메시지 화면, 카페 달 낮은 조명, 겹치는 안부와 작은 웃음
- Energy level: low-medium, careful, reoriented

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: dim piano chords, cafe room tone, muted low strings, warm-dry reverb
- Tempo feel: held conversational pulse
- Vocal distance: medium-close, face-to-face
- Arrangement: keep the room warmer than the melody, never celebratory

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: send screen, low cafe light, overlapping greeting, table air
- Point of view: chosen re-entry into a conversation that cannot be shortcut
- Words to avoid: back together, perfect timing, destiny restart, hearing truth
- Do not overstate: This is chosen re-entry, not full repair.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No homecoming anthem, no nostalgia gloss, no restored-love climax.

## Delivery

The song should feel like two people entering the same room with less certainty and more honesty.

```text
Restrained Korean relationship track, dim piano chords, cafe room tone, muted low strings, warm-dry reverb, medium-close vocal. Mood: short message sent with hesitation, low-lit cafe, overlapping hello, small laugh that does not erase the gap, careful chosen meeting. Avoid back-together language, nostalgia glow, destiny restart, hearing-return cues, big chorus release, and famous song imitation.
```
