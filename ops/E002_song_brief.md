# E002 Song Brief

## episode

- id: E002
- title anchor: 서점에서의 충격
- function: relationship theme
- emotional thesis: 이어폰을 뚫고 들어온 첫 예외

## core feeling

- chorus emotion line: 닫아 둔 귀를 지나, 처음으로 한 사람의 결만 또렷했다
- seasonal air: late-spring bookstore afternoon, paper scent, glass-filtered light
- energy level: restrained wonder, sudden clarity, suspended shock

## sound direction

- sonic palette: transparent piano, very light strings, subtle air texture, glass-like brightness
- tempo feel: slow suspended pulse, as if time briefly stops in an aisle
- vocal distance: close emotional vocal, still careful and stunned
- arrangement rule: the chorus may open slightly, but never become triumphant or fate-heavy

## lyrical direction

- dominant image family: bookstore window, paper, dust in sunlight, a chair after someone leaves, glass clarity through noise
- point of view: near-Seojun perception, but with relationship-theme widening
- words to avoid: first-sight love, miracle, destiny, forever, obvious soulmate language
- plot points not to overstate: do not explain Arin's exception as a rule; keep it as an embodied shock

## short prose summary

This is the song of the first crack in Seojun's closed world. It should feel like a clear note passing through a locked door before he knows whose hand opened it.
