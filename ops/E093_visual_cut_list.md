# E093 Visual Cut List - 편안함과 사랑

## Episode Identity

- Episode: E093
- Song: E093 theme placeholder
- Emotional thesis: 덜 불편한 관계가 반드시 더 사랑인 것은 아니다.
- Cut count: 8

## Master Look Prompt

Cinematic live-action Korean table-detail stills, hands and cups emphasized over faces, pauses before speech, empty space after reply, soft gray-amber palette, realistic faces, no anime, no homecoming atmosphere, no logos, no readable signage.

## Tool Plan

- Style exploration: Midjourney
- Production stills: Nano Banana
- Hero-shot enhancement: Imagen for the table-gap frame if needed

## Cut List

1. Hands and cups: a table frame led by hands, cup rims, and distance.
2. Pause before speech: lips stop before the line lands.
3. Neutral eye line: eyes stay careful, not romantic.
4. Reply lands: reaction is small and inward.
5. Space after answer: more empty tabletop than expected.
6. No bridge: nothing in frame visually “connects” the two.
7. Chairline: chairs suggest arrangement without closeness.
8. Leftover room: the room remains more present than the relationship.

## Continuity Anchors

- Motif: table gap, cup rims, paused mouth, leftover room
- Comfort rule: not equal to truth
- Prohibition: no warm soft-focus pair framing, no “safe home” image, no duet symmetry

## House Notes

The frame should quietly refuse to turn comfort into a prize.
