# E009 Song Brief

## episode

- id: E009
- title anchor: 마음의 비밀
- function: Seojun inner monologue
- emotional thesis: 믿고 싶은데, 믿으면 다칠까 봐 망설인다

## core feeling

- chorus emotion line: 믿는 쪽으로 기울 때마다, 오래된 상처가 먼저 손목을 잡았다
- seasonal air: cool evening spring air with a half-open window
- energy level: bruised, careful, inward, unsettled

## sound direction

- sonic palette: sparse piano, low strings, cold air texture, restrained pulse
- tempo feel: slow, hesitant, like stopping mid-step
- vocal distance: restrained close male vocal, emotionally withheld
- arrangement rule: allow small lifts of ache, but pull them back before they become melodrama

## lyrical direction

- dominant image family: wrist caught mid-reach, half-open window, cool air, room edge, unfinished step
- point of view: Seojun inward voice
- words to avoid: self-pity excess, revenge, grand trauma speech, destiny wording
- plot points not to overstate: do not explain the full trauma; let fear appear through bodily hesitation

## short prose summary

This is the song of someone who realizes the difficult part is no longer being interested, but allowing that interest to turn into trust. The track should feel like a hand stopping just before it reaches the doorknob.

## Suno-ready prompt

```text
Korean introspective emotional ballad, restrained male vocal, sparse piano and low strings, cool spring evening air through a half-open window, hesitation between trust and pain, intimate but guarded, cinematic and bruised, no dramatic breakdown, no direct trauma exposition
```

## guardrails

- fear and tenderness must coexist
- keep the song inward and physically felt, not abstract
- the emotional job is hesitation, not collapse
