# E081 Song Brief - 침묵 이후

## Identity

- Episode: E081
- Song function: Ending reflection
- Emotional thesis: 세상은 아직 소리를 냈지만, 그는 그것을 뜻으로 묶지 못했다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "세상은 멈추지 않았고, 나는 그 소리를 더 이상 문장으로 읽지 못했다."
- Seasonal air: empty classroom, paper movement, autumn window light
- Energy level: low, hollow, unfinished

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: room air, paper movement, low campus noise, incomplete motif dust
- Tempo feel: slow routine continuing
- Vocal distance: close but passive
- Arrangement: leave ordinary noise alive without turning it into a melody

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: empty class, paper hand, stopped earphones, hallway continuing
- Point of view: Seojun inside bare silence
- Words to avoid: peace, recovered, hearing again, mature silence
- Do not overstate: This is Stage 4, not completion.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No recovered melody and no complete-peace tone.

## Delivery

The song should make silence feel unfinished and livable, not solved.

```text
Minimal Korean post-loss reflection track, room air, paper movement, low campus noise, incomplete motif dust, close passive vocal. Mood: empty classroom, stopped earphones, autumn window light, ordinary hallway continuing while meaning no longer assembles. Avoid peace, recovered melody, hearing-again lyrics, mature-silence framing, and famous song imitation.
```
