# E083 Song Brief - 남겨진 습관

## Identity

- Episode: E083
- Song function: Seojun inner monologue
- Emotional thesis: 사라진 건 소리였고, 남은 건 맞추려는 버릇이었다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "들리지 않는데도, 나는 아직 맞추려는 손부터 움직였다."
- Seasonal air: two-cup order stopped, deleted message, empty next seat
- Energy level: low, repetitive, uneasy

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: imperfect repetition, finger tap, dim room pulse, nearly absent motif
- Tempo feel: habit loop that keeps failing
- Vocal distance: close and dry
- Arrangement: repeat a small gesture rhythm, but never resolve it

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: stopped hand, deleted message, side glance, empty seat
- Point of view: habit after ability collapse
- Words to avoid: ghost Arin, vision, sign from her, hearing returns
- Do not overstate: What remains is habit, not power.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No ghost vocal, no transparent-memory effect, no returning theme.

## Delivery

The song should make an old reflex feel useless but still alive.

```text
Dry Korean inner-monologue track, imperfect repetition, finger tap, dim room pulse, nearly absent motif, close restrained vocal. Mood: ordering two cups then stopping, deleted message, side glance to an empty seat, an old reflex that no longer works. Avoid ghost Arin, vision lyrics, sign-from-her framing, hearing-returns cues, and famous song imitation.
```
