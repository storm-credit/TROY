# E113 Song Brief - 아린의 선택

## Identity

- Episode: E113
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 사랑은 상대를 잃지 않기 위해 내가 나를 잃지 않는 일이다

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "선배가 좋아도, 나는 나를 잃고 싶지 않아"
- Seasonal air: 맑은 공기, 다음 학기, 과제와 밴드, 살아남기 위한 호흡
- Energy level: low, clear, humane

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: airy piano, restrained strings, soft low tone, no swell
- Tempo feel: calm and forward
- Vocal distance: medium-close, assured but gentle
- Arrangement: let the declaration breathe

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: open air, school planning, moving forward without shrinking
- Point of view: Arin affirming selfhood inside love
- Words to avoid: sacrifice, breakup alarm, possessive love, destiny
- Do not overstate: This is not an ending, it is a self-preserving choice.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No sacrificial romance, no relationship panic, no finality hook.

## Delivery

The song should feel like an honest self-guarding line inside affection.

```text
Korean clear relationship track, airy piano, restrained strings, soft low tone, medium-close assured vocal. Mood: fresh campus air, next semester plans, band practice, and a self-preserving statement that love cannot erase identity. Avoid sacrifice framing, breakup alarm, possessive language, destiny language, and famous song imitation.
```
