# E053 Song Brief - 익숙한 행복

## Identity

- Episode: E053
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 익숙한 행복은 때로 질문을 잠들게 했다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "편안해서, 묻지 않은 것들이 조용히 잠들었다."
- Seasonal air: repeated cafe routine, summer loop, easy warmth
- Energy level: mid-light, cozy and slightly sleepy

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: warm cafe groove, soft electric piano, light percussion, low bass
- Tempo feel: comfortable loop
- Vocal distance: relaxed and close
- Arrangement: repeat a small motif until it feels safe, then add a faint low note

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: same cup, same path, closed question, easy laugh
- Point of view: relationship routine with hidden risk
- Words to avoid: perfect routine, forever, no problems, destiny
- Do not overstate: This is not ominous yet.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No imitation. Make the loop feel original and lived-in.

## Delivery

The song should feel cozy enough that not asking seems natural.

```text
Cozy Korean cafe-romance theme, warm electric piano, light percussion, low bass, soft guitar color, relaxed close vocal. Mood: familiar happiness becoming routine, same cup, same path, easy laugh, questions quietly falling asleep. Avoid perfect routine, forever, destiny, no-problems language, and obvious foreshadowing.
```
