# E082 Song Brief - 익숙하지 않은 평온

## Identity

- Episode: E082
- Song function: Ending reflection
- Emotional thesis: 평온은 왔지만, 아직 그 평온을 믿을 수는 없었다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "조용해서 덜 아팠고, 조용해서 아무것도 확인할 수 없었다."
- Seasonal air: Cafe Dal afternoon, empty opposite chair, low warm light
- Energy level: low, calm but hollow

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: low ambient room, distant street, sparse felt piano, no melodic return
- Tempo feel: suspended afternoon
- Vocal distance: close, uncertain
- Arrangement: let warmth enter without becoming healing

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: empty chair, cup not cold, afternoon table, breathing room
- Point of view: unfamiliar calm after loss
- Words to avoid: healed, peace completed, replaced, better without you
- Do not overstate: Calm and vacancy must coexist.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No healing-ad tone and no romanticized absence.

## Delivery

The song should let calm feel suspicious, not triumphant.

```text
Quiet Korean autumn reflection track, low ambient room, distant street, sparse felt piano, no melodic return, close uncertain vocal. Mood: Cafe Dal afternoon table, empty opposite chair, cup not cold, low warm light, calm that feels unfamiliar and untrusted. Avoid healed language, completed peace, replaced-you framing, healing-ad tone, and famous song imitation.
```
