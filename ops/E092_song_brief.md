# E092 Song Brief - 과거의 해석

## Identity

- Episode: E092
- Song function: Seojun inner monologue
- Emotional thesis: 잊을 수 없던 사람이 아니라, 잊지 못하게 만든 해석이 다시 눈앞에 섰다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "남은 건 그 사람이 아니라, 내가 오래 붙잡은 뜻이었다."
- Seasonal air: 카페 달 창가, 닫힌 컵, 늦은 오후의 건조한 빛
- Energy level: low, tense, analytic

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: broken piano phrase, dry string texture, distant room tone, unresolved short harmony
- Tempo feel: stop-start, thought interrupting itself
- Vocal distance: close, inward, unsmoothed
- Arrangement: keep phrases clipped; do not let the line bloom

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: window divide, untouched cup, unfinished sentence, returning afterimage
- Point of view: examining the wound without letting it become nostalgia
- Words to avoid: healed, closure, first love returned, fate
- Do not overstate: Yu-bin is not the answer; the point is the pattern.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No grand nostalgia, no confession warmth, no cure-through-conversation framing.

## Delivery

The song should feel like noticing that the memory you trusted was partly built by your own hand.

```text
Dry Korean introspective track, broken piano phrase, restrained string texture, distant room tone, unresolved short harmony, close unsmoothed vocal. Mood: Cafe Dal window divide, untouched cup, unfinished sentence, late dry light, memory examined without nostalgia. Avoid healed language, closure framing, first-love-return energy, fate cues, cure-through-conversation tone, and famous song imitation.
```
