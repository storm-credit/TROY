# E052 Song Brief - 함께 있는 미래의 환상

## Identity

- Episode: E052
- Song function: Seojun inner monologue
- Emotional thesis: 내일을 그리느라 오늘의 너를 조금 놓쳤다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "내일의 창문을 보다가, 오늘의 네 표정을 놓쳤다."
- Seasonal air: warm reflection, future projection, present daylight
- Energy level: low-to-mid, hopeful but unstable

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: warm electric piano, distant pad, light bass, soft shimmer
- Tempo feel: floating walking pulse
- Vocal distance: close, dreamy, then slightly clearer
- Arrangement: let imagined sections feel warmer than present sections

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: window reflection, future path, present table, light ahead
- Point of view: Seojun projecting forward
- Words to avoid: marriage, forever, destiny, promised future
- Do not overstate: The future is imagination, not mutual agreement.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No famous ballad references. Keep projection and present-day contrast original.

## Delivery

The song should sound like hope getting one step ahead of listening.

```text
Dreamy but restrained Korean inner monologue, warm electric piano, distant pad, light bass, soft shimmer, close vocal that gradually becomes clearer. Mood: imagining a future together and missing the present expression, window reflection, future path, present table, light ahead. Avoid marriage, forever, destiny, promised future, and grand romantic certainty.
```
