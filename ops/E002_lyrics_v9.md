## Rationale (post-script, not pasted to Suno)

### Diagnostic (E002 v8 → v9 gap)

E002 v8 underperforms vs E001 on six measurable craft axes:

1. **Hook latency.** v8 opens with "오늘은 한 사람을 너무 오래 봐 버렸어" — 14 음절, observational-confessional. The listener has to PARSE the line before feeling it. E001's hook is universally pre-parsed (잘 지내냐 → 잘 지낸다 lie). v9 restores 0-second latency with "안 본 척 한 번 더 돌아봤어" — every Korean has performed this exact micro-act. The line IS the emotion.
2. **Lock-line conceptuality.** "처음으로 한 사람의 결만 또렷했어" carries a literary noun ("결") that listeners can admire but cannot echo. E001's "내 귀부터 닫고 살았어" can be hummed after one pass. v9's "한 사람이 자꾸 눈에 담겼어" is 10 음절, ㄷ-family alliteration (담겼), passive register (matches E001's closure psychology — things HAPPEN to me, not I DO things), and universally singable.
3. **Verse texture.** v8 uses "지나간 자리 / 한 칸 / 그쪽" — abstract spatial. v9 uses 가방끈 / 시계 / 신발 / 거울 / 물 한 잔 — domestic objects that match E001's 이불·거울·창문 register.
4. **Bridge subversion.** v8 = explicit ambivalence ("좋은 건지 무서운 건지"). v9 follows E001's self-doubt-as-discovery model: "안 보려고 본 건지도 몰라 / 못 본 척 하려고 두 번 본 건지도 몰라" — the verb of avoidance becomes the verb of looking.
5. **Chorus tail.** v8 ends each chorus with "가만히, 아주 가만히" — precious / curated. v9 ends with "같은 자리에 두 번 멈췄어" — declarative, conversational, lets the lock line breathe.
6. **Final lift mechanics.** v8 lift "처음으로, 한 사람만 또렷했어" loses a noun by compression. v9 lift "한 사람만 눈에 담겨 있었어" performs E001's past-perfect closure move (담기다 + 었어 → 담겨 있었어) — same lock verb stem, micro-shift to past-perfect, structurally identical to E001's "닫고 살았어" → "닫아 두었어" lift.

### First-line hook design

**Chosen**: "안 본 척 한 번 더 돌아봤어" (10 음절, 한 호흡)
**Rejected**:
- "한 사람한테 눈이 자꾸 갔어" — closer to v8 problem (described, not enacted)
- "고개 돌리려다 한 박자 늦었어" — too dense in 음절 cluster, weak surface-cover
- "보지 말자고 다짐을 두 번 했어" — internal self-instruction, no body
- "안 보려는 척 가방끈을 만졌어" — strong but object-loaded for line 1

Why the chosen line works: it mirrors E001's craft signature exactly. E001 line 1 = a LIE you tell with your mouth (잘 지낸다고 답했어). E002 line 1 = a LIE you tell with your body (안 본 척 한 번 더 돌아봤어). Both are universal Korean self-betrayals performed in public. Both are emotionally COMPLETE at the end of line 1 — the song doesn't need to set anything up, the wound is already showing. "안 본 척" pre-establishes the closure register (this is someone who is TRYING not to feel), and "한 번 더 돌아봤어" is the first micro-crack — exactly the Phase 1 E002 anchor.

The phrase "한 번 더" then re-appears in pre-chorus ("안 본 척 한 번 더 시계를 봤어") and bridge ("두 번 본 건지도") and final chorus ("두 번 멈췄어" callback) — building melodic memory the way E001 builds the dental motif.

### Chorus emotion line + placement

**Lock**: "한 사람이 자꾸 눈에 담겼어" — 10 음절, breath unit 4+6.
- Alliteration: "자꾸" (ㅈ) + "담겼" (ㄷ) — soft-consonant friendly for alto.
- Passive verb ("담기다") matches E001 closure-psychology — things happen to the speaker. This is critical for Phase 1: the speaker has not yet authorized the feeling, it is arriving against their closure.
- Parallel to E001: 닫고(ㄷ)/살았어 ↔ 담겼(ㄷ)/어. Both ㄷ-family verb stems on the lock noun.
- Verbatim 3x (chorus 1, 2, final lines 1-2). Final chorus line 6 = sanctioned 1-variant lift.

**Lift**: "한 사람만 눈에 담겨 있었어" — 11 음절.
- 담겼어 → 담겨 있었어 (past-perfect resting state). Same verb stem, micro-shifted to closure. Structurally identical to E001's 닫고 살았어 → 닫아 두었어 move.
- "이/가" → "만" — narrows from "one [among many]" to "only one." The chorus's "얼굴들이 너무 많아서" plurality dissolves to singularity. This is the E002 anchor's musical destination.

Chorus line 1: "오늘은 얼굴들이 너무 많아서" — declarative stake-setting line, structurally identical to E001's "마음들이 너무 많아서". Both Phase 1 episodes open chorus with "너무 많아서" plurality → singularity-of-closure. Intentional formal echo so the two episodes feel like siblings in the same first chapter.

- 봄/계절 직접 선언: 0건.
- 도구 직접 호출 (이어폰/헤드폰): 0건.

### Standout lines (5 hand-picked, in order of strength)

1. **"안 본 척 한 번 더 돌아봤어"** (verse 1 line 1) — 0-second hook. Every Korean listener has done this exact micro-act. Self-betrayal performed in one line, surface-cover (안 본 척) and rupture (한 번 더 돌아봤어) coexist. The emotional event is COMPLETE in line 1 — the rest of the song is the speaker accounting for it.
2. **"한 사람이 자꾸 눈에 담겼어"** (chorus lock) — Listener can echo after one play. ㄷ-family alliteration, passive register, conversational. The "자꾸" carries the entire Phase 1 ambivalence (the speaker did not authorize this, it kept happening).
3. **"어쩌면 나는 안 보려고 본 건지도 몰라 / 못 본 척 하려고 두 번 본 건지도 몰라"** (bridge lines 1-2) — Self-doubt-as-discovery in the E001 mode. The verb of avoidance (안 보려고) collapses into the verb of looking (본 건지도) — same syntactic move E001 uses for "듣고 싶었던 건지도 몰라". The closure was the looking, in disguise.
4. **"나는 문을 닫은 채로 자리만 비워 뒀어"** (bridge line 4) — Anti-destiny line. The empty seat exists but the speaker authored it by closing the door, not because fate prefilled it. This is the v9 line that protects against the 운명-cliche slide that v8's "한 칸이 비어 있었던 것처럼" risked.
5. **"한 사람만 눈에 담겨 있었어"** (final lift) — Past-perfect closure of the lock verb. 담겼어 → 담겨 있었어 = E001's 닫고 살았어 → 닫아 두었어 structural twin. Restrained, declarative, lands musically at the end of female-alto delivery.

### Removed from v8 (what got cut and why)

- **"처음으로 한 사람의 결만 또렷했어"** (chorus lock) — Literary noun "결" risks sensory-origin slide AND is 14 음절 (un-singable). Replaced with "한 사람이 자꾸 눈에 담겼어" (10 음절, passive, ㄷ-alliterated, echo-able).
- **"빛이 한 번 거기서 멎은 것 같아"** (chorus line 3) — Beautiful as a poem, dead as a sung line. Imagery that doesn't connect to body. Replaced with "이름도 모르는데 또렷했어" — declarative, retains the v8 "이름이 없는데도" insight in singable form.
- **"오늘 하루가 그쪽으로 기울었어"** (chorus line 5) — Abstract directional metaphor, Suno has no template for it. Replaced with "같은 길을 두 번 걸어 봤어" — concrete repeated act, body in space.
- **"한 칸이 비어 있었던 것처럼"** (bridge) — Destiny-adjacent (prefilled seat). Replaced with "한 사람이 오늘 내 안에 들어왔는데 / 나는 문을 닫은 채로 자리만 비워 뒀어" — speaker authors the gap via closure, anti-destiny.
- **"가만히, 아주 가만히"** (chorus line 6) — Over-curated tail, breaks the conversational register. Cut entirely.
- **"이게 좋은 건지 무서운 건지 / 나도 잘 모르겠어"** (bridge opener) — Explicit ambivalence (telling not showing). Replaced with self-doubt-as-discovery pattern.
- **"지나간 자리를 다시 한 번 걸어 봤어 / 그 자리만 공기가 조금 다른 것 같아 / 같은 길인데 같은 길이 아니었어"** (verse 2 of v8) — Three abstract spatial lines in a row. v9 verse 2 keeps the "same place, different feel" insight but channels it through concrete domestic objects (신발, 거울, 물 한 잔) — same emotional payload, bodied delivery.
- **"오늘은 한 사람을 너무 오래 봐 버렸어"** (hook) — Confessional but parsing-required. Replaced with "안 본 척 한 번 더 돌아봤어" (act, not description).
- **"가만히" + "결" + "빛" + "기울었어"** quartet — collectively shifted v8 toward poet-register and away from singer-register. All cut.
