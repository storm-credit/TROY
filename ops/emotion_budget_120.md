# Emotion Budget: 120 Episodes

## 1. Purpose

120화 전체의 감정 고저와 회복량을 관리한다.

## 2. Rule

- tension without recovery causes numbness
- happiness without shadow causes flatness
- grief without modulation causes melodrama

## 3. Phase Budget

### Phase 1

- mode: curiosity
- pressure: low to medium
- recovery: high

### Phase 2

- mode: mutual approach
- pressure: medium
- recovery: medium-high

### Phase 3

- mode: fullness before fracture
- pressure: medium-high
- recovery: medium

### Phase 4

- mode: rupture
- pressure: high
- recovery: low

### Phase 5

- mode: reinterpretation
- pressure: medium
- recovery: medium

### Phase 6

- mode: lucid farewell
- pressure: medium-high but clear
- recovery: low, replaced by residue
