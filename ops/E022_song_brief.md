# E022 Song Brief - 익숙해진 목소리

## Identity

- Episode: E022
- Song function: Seojun inner monologue
- Emotional thesis: 익숙해진다는 건 안심보다 먼저 두려움을 데려온다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "네 목소리가 없는 시간에도, 나는 그쪽을 기다렸다."
- Seasonal air: quiet classroom, thin late-afternoon window light
- Energy level: low, intimate, slightly anxious

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: close piano, soft guitar, faint ambient phone-light texture
- Tempo feel: slow and observant
- Vocal distance: intimate, withheld, nearly spoken in places
- Arrangement: keep the chorus small and inward

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: voice, window, empty desk, phone light, waiting air
- Point of view: Seojun noticing familiarity as danger
- Words to avoid: ringtone hook, addiction, obsession, certainty
- Do not overstate: Voice is not the same as rhythm or truth.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No named songs, no ringtone-style imitation. Keep the motif human and subtle.

## Delivery

The song should make the absence of a voice feel like an ordinary room becoming incomplete.

```text
Intimate Korean indie ballad, close piano, soft guitar, faint ambient phone-light texture, slow observant tempo, withheld close vocal. Mood: becoming used to someone's voice and feeling afraid of that comfort, quiet classroom, empty desk, late-afternoon window light. Lyrics should use voice, waiting air, phone glow without readable text, and a room that feels incomplete. Avoid obsession, certainty, ringtone hooks, and direct supernatural explanation.
```
