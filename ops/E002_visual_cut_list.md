# E002 Visual Cut List

## episode

- id: E002
- title anchor: 서점에서의 충격
- song function: Relationship theme
- MV type: anomaly encounter / first exception
- recommended still count: 10

## visual thesis

조용한 서점 안에서 처음으로 회색 세계가 투명하게 흔들린다.

## master look prompt

```text
Cinematic 16:9 still for episode 2 of a Korean campus romance.
Same 22-year-old Korean male student from episode 1: charcoal hoodie, wired earphones, guarded expression.
Introduce a 21-year-old Korean female student with long dark hair, light blouse or soft spring cardigan, transparent warm presence, quiet intelligence.
Location: university bookstore with wooden shelves, old paper atmosphere, soft afternoon sunlight through glass.
Visual contrast: Seojun remains gray-blue and guarded, Arin introduces clearer, warmer, more transparent light.
No text, no logo, no watermark.
```

## tool plan

- style lock from E001 via image reference
- production stills: Nano Banana
- one hero upgrade frame for first reveal: Imagen optional

## cut list

### cut 1. 서점 외부 진입

- purpose: 방공호 같은 공간 소개
- shot: exterior to interior transition
- motion intent: slow approach

prompt:
```text
University bookstore entrance in late spring afternoon, soft glass reflections, a quiet Korean male student entering alone with wired earphones, calm refuge-like mood, cinematic 16:9
```

### cut 2. 서가 사이를 걷는 서준

- purpose: 익숙한 안전지대의 리듬
- shot: medium tracking
- motion intent: side tracking

prompt:
```text
Korean male student walking between wooden bookstore shelves, old paper atmosphere, quiet and controlled body language, muted gray-blue palette, cinematic realism, 16:9
```

### cut 3. 책을 찾는 손

- purpose: 규칙적이고 닫힌 일상
- shot: insert close-up
- motion intent: static

prompt:
```text
Close-up of a hand reaching for a marketing textbook on a bookstore shelf, charcoal sleeve, warm wood shelves, controlled and habitual motion, 16:9
```

### cut 4. 첫 이상 징후

- purpose: 소리의 침입 전조
- shot: medium close-up on Seojun
- motion intent: sudden stillness

prompt:
```text
Medium close-up of a guarded Korean male student suddenly freezing in a quiet bookstore, eyes shifting as if hearing something impossible, afternoon light, emotional shock beginning, cinematic 16:9
```

### cut 5. 창가의 아린 첫 reveal

- purpose: 아린의 존재를 투명한 빛으로 소개
- shot: hero medium-wide
- motion intent: gentle push-in

prompt:
```text
A young Korean woman sitting by a bookstore window reading a novel, long dark hair, soft light blouse or pale cardigan, afternoon sunlight and dust motes around her, transparent calm presence, warm but delicate, cinematic 16:9
```

### cut 6. 책장을 넘기는 손과 빛

- purpose: 감정의 변주를 직접 설명하지 않고 보여주기
- shot: insert close-up
- motion intent: very slow drift

prompt:
```text
Close-up of a young woman's hand turning a book page in warm window light, dust floating in the air, soft elegant movement, subtle emotional brightness, cinematic 16:9
```

### cut 7. 서가 너머 응시

- purpose: 서준의 시선과 끌림
- shot: over-shelf composition
- motion intent: slow rack focus

prompt:
```text
View through bookstore shelves toward a young woman by the window, a quiet male student partially hidden in foreground watching, emotional distance collapsing, layered composition, cinematic 16:9
```

### cut 8. 눈이 마주치는 순간

- purpose: 규칙이 깨지는 정면 충돌
- shot: alternating close-up or centered medium
- motion intent: hold

prompt:
```text
Moment of eye contact between a guarded Korean male student and a warm, curious Korean female student in a quiet bookstore, sunlight between them, tension and surprise, intimate cinematic still, 16:9
```

### cut 9. 그녀가 스쳐 지나가는 순간

- purpose: 가장 가까운 통과, 가장 큰 흔들림
- shot: side close-medium
- motion intent: soft passing blur

prompt:
```text
A young Korean woman passing close by a frozen male student in a bookstore aisle, soft spring scent implied through movement, his world briefly brightened, elegant side composition, cinematic 16:9
```

### cut 10. 창가와 빈자리 엔딩

- purpose: 소리가 사라진 뒤의 허망함
- shot: wide still with empty chair
- motion intent: slow fade

prompt:
```text
Empty bookstore window seat after someone has just left, late afternoon light, quiet male student standing still nearby, emotional afterimage, transparent warmth fading back into gray silence, cinematic 16:9
```

## carry-over notes

- Seojun should visually remain consistent with episode 1
- Arin should feel warmer and clearer, but not fantasy-like
- bookstore light must feel transparent, not golden-romantic
- final cut should leave absence, not fulfillment
