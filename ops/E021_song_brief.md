# E021 Song Brief - 가까워지는 거리

## Identity

- Episode: E021
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 가까워짐은 큰 사건보다 반복되는 보폭으로 먼저 온다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "같은 길을 걷다 보니, 하루의 기준이 조금 바뀌었다."
- Seasonal air: early summer approaching, soft campus afternoon, light shadows
- Energy level: low-to-mid, warm accumulation

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: soft acoustic pulse, clean piano, light campus ambience, faint brushed percussion
- Tempo feel: gentle walking tempo
- Vocal distance: close and relaxed, not confessional
- Arrangement: gradual layering, no dramatic lift

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: same path, side-by-side steps, classroom door, afternoon shadow
- Point of view: relationship space through Seojun's quiet noticing
- Words to avoid: destiny, couple, forever, perfect distance
- Do not overstate: This is routine closeness, not relationship confirmation.

## Safe Prompt Rule

Do not imitate real songs or artists. Translate only routine closeness, campus air, and soft walking rhythm.

## Delivery

The song should feel like a person quietly becoming part of the day's measurement.

```text
Gentle Korean campus romance theme, soft acoustic pulse, clean piano, faint brushed percussion, light campus ambience, close relaxed vocal. Mood: two people naturally spending more time together, same path, side-by-side steps, afternoon shadows, ordinary closeness. Lyrics should use images of a classroom door, repeated walks, and a day measured by someone's distance. Avoid destiny, forever, couple language, and dramatic confession.
```
