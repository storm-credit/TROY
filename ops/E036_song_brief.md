# E036 Song Brief - 연애 시작

## Identity

- Episode: E036
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 시작은 문이 열리는 소리였고, 그 안에는 빛과 그림자가 같이 있었다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "문이 열리자, 여름빛과 그림자가 함께 들어왔다."
- Seasonal air: early summer threshold, named relationship, warm air with a shadow
- Energy level: mid, warm but restrained

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: warmer electric piano, light bass groove, airy percussion, soft guitar shimmer
- Tempo feel: gentle summer pulse
- Vocal distance: clear, warmer, still not triumphant
- Arrangement: more movement than E031-E035 but with a faint minor-color shadow

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: door, summer light, first promise, shadow under brightness
- Point of view: relationship threshold
- Words to avoid: happily ever after, destiny, forever, solved, official couple anthem
- Do not overstate: Relationship start is new terrain, not resolution.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No artist imitation or glossy idol-pop request. Keep it cinematic and restrained.

## Delivery

The song should feel like the beginning of summer with a small shadow at the edge.

```text
Warm restrained Korean campus romance theme, warmer electric piano, light bass groove, airy percussion, soft guitar shimmer, clear warm vocal. Mood: official relationship beginning, summer light entering through an open door with a small shadow at the edge. Lyrics should use door, summer air, first promise, brightness and shadow. Avoid happily-ever-after, destiny, forever, solved, and triumphant couple anthem.
```
