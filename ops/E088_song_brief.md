# E088 Song Brief - 아린 없는 세계

## Identity

- Episode: E088
- Song function: Ending reflection
- Emotional thesis: 없는 사람을 지우지 않은 채, 하루는 다른 균형으로 계속됐다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "네가 없어서 사라진 세계가 아니라, 네가 없음을 안고 계속되는 세계였다."
- Seasonal air: respectful friends, half-open door, small daily care
- Energy level: low, breathing, grounded

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: low ambient room, small daily percussion, restrained strings, no ghost vocal
- Tempo feel: ordinary life finding a different balance
- Vocal distance: close but less collapsed
- Arrangement: let daily sounds carry absence without romanticizing it

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: snack set down, friend nearby, half-open door, campus continuing
- Point of view: Seojun respecting Arin's absence and life
- Words to avoid: replacement, erased, you were everything, ghost, new love
- Do not overstate: Arin is absent, not reduced to a function.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No ghost vocal and no romanticized absence.

## Delivery

The song should let the world breathe around absence without making it disposable.

```text
Grounded Korean autumn reflection track, low ambient room, small daily percussion, restrained strings, no ghost vocal, close but steadier vocal. Mood: snack set down quietly, friend at respectful distance, half-open door, campus continuing, a world carrying absence without erasing it. Avoid replacement lyrics, erased-you framing, you-were-everything melodrama, ghost imagery, new-love hints, and famous song imitation.
```
