## Cut List

1. Cloudy morning: the world is open but muted.
2. Doorway detail: frame led by handle, edge, and line.
3. Stair line: vertical geometry suggests a climb without triumph.
4. Hand near handle: hand pauses before touching.
5. Face before crossing: expression shows readiness, not certainty.
6. Breath held: a beat of stillness before movement.
7. One small step: movement begins without crescendo.
8. No smile resolution: face stays serious and present.
9. Path not fully shown: the beyond-space remains partly hidden.
10. Unresolved threshold: final frame is a hinge, not an opening ceremony.

## Continuity Anchors

- Motif: handle, stair line, paused hand, cloudy threshold
- Threshold rule: hinge, not payoff
- Prohibition: no bright rebirth wide shot, no hopeful walk-off, no ability-return symbolism

## House Notes

The last frame should make the next movement possible without pretending it has already arrived.
