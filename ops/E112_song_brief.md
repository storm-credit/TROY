# E112 Song Brief - 말로 하는 고백

## Identity

- Episode: E112
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 고백은 알아내는 것이 아니라 책임지는 것이다

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "듣고 알아내서 하는 고백이 아니라, 모른 채로도 책임지겠다는 고백"
- Seasonal air: 카페 달 조명, 정직한 간격, 두 손으로 감싼 컵
- Energy level: low-medium, intimate, clear

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: dim piano chords, soft room tone, restrained low strings, warm-dry reverb
- Tempo feel: measured confession
- Vocal distance: medium-close, face-to-face
- Arrangement: keep the confession plain enough to carry responsibility

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: low light, cups, pauses, eyes held steady
- Point of view: Seojun speaking without overexplaining
- Words to avoid: fate, perfect timing, closure, victory
- Do not overstate: This is the start of responsibility, not the end of tension.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No confession climax, no romantic swell, no closure glow.

## Delivery

The song should feel like a quiet sentence that takes responsibility for itself.

```text
Korean intimate relationship track, dim piano chords, soft room tone, restrained low strings, warm-dry reverb, medium-close steady vocal. Mood: cafe lamp light, a cup held in both hands, a slow and honest confession that does not rush to solve anything. Avoid fate language, perfect-timing framing, closure glow, victory anthem tone, and famous song imitation.
```
