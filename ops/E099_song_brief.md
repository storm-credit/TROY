# E099 Song Brief - 다시 만나기 전

## Identity

- Episode: E099
- Song function: Ending reflection
- Emotional thesis: 아직 흔들리지만, 그 흔들림이 곧 돌아가야 한다는 뜻은 아니었다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "다시 만나고 싶은 마음보다, 같은 방식으로 망치지 않겠다는 다짐이 먼저였다."
- Seasonal air: 병렬 준비, 별도 동선, 문 앞에서 멈춘 발, 흐린 가을 아침
- Energy level: low, taut, waiting

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: cool piano chords, incomplete cadence, shallow drone, intermittent spatial noise
- Tempo feel: held breath before contact
- Vocal distance: medium-close, careful, unromantic
- Arrangement: preserve tension; do not let the refrain open into hope

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: threshold, separate route, paused step, cold morning air
- Point of view: preparing to meet without claiming
- Words to avoid: soon again, we return, meant to be, my person
- Do not overstate: This is not the beginning of reunion.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No duet mood, no lyrical bloom, no destiny synchronization.

## Delivery

The song should sound like discipline under longing.

```text
Tense Korean autumn threshold track, cool piano chords, incomplete cadence, shallow drone, intermittent spatial noise, medium-close careful vocal. Mood: separate preparation, cold morning air, paused foot at a doorway, held breath before contact, discipline stronger than longing. Avoid reunion framing, destiny synchronization, duet warmth, lyrical bloom, and famous song imitation.
```
