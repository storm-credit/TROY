# E109 Song Brief - 거리와 예의

## Identity

- Episode: E109
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 가까움은 밀착이 아니라, 말할 공간을 남기는 예의가 될 수 있었다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "붙잡지 않는 한 걸음 뒤에서, 우리는 처음으로 숨 쉴 자리를 남겼다."
- Seasonal air: 인문관 복도, 한 걸음 뒤, `오늘은 여기까지`, 옅은 오후 그림자
- Energy level: low, respectful, mature

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: muted piano, corridor air, soft brushed pulse, low sustained tone
- Tempo feel: one-step-back cadence
- Vocal distance: medium, calm and observant
- Arrangement: keep the restraint active and caring, not empty

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: corridor distance, pause before goodbye, room left for speech, held space
- Point of view: closeness redefined through restraint and trust
- Words to avoid: chase, obsession as love, can't let go, dramatic longing
- Do not overstate: Restraint is trust, not fading affection.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No chase melody, no desperate hook, no possessive language.

## Delivery

The song should feel like stepping back without emotionally leaving.

```text
Korean mature relationship theme, muted piano, corridor air, soft brushed pulse, low sustained tone, medium calm vocal. Mood: humanities-hall hallway, one step back to leave room, accepting stop without protest, learning that space itself can be care. Avoid chase imagery, obsession-as-love framing, possessive language, dramatic longing swell, and famous song imitation.
```
