# E065 Song Brief - 어긋난 타이밍

## Identity

- Episode: E065
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 우리는 같은 문장에 닿으려 했지만, 늘 몇 초씩 늦었다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "같은 곳에 닿아도, 우리는 같은 순간이 아니었다."
- Seasonal air: elevator door, opposite crosswalk, cafe threshold, late phone light
- Energy level: mid-low, restless but restrained

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: offset pulse, delayed vocal double, soft ticking percussion, unresolved guitar note
- Tempo feel: cross-cut timing, slightly late
- Vocal distance: two close voices not perfectly aligned
- Arrangement: let the echo arrive a fraction late

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: closing elevator, crosswalk, cafe door, late message
- Point of view: relationship timing mismatch
- Words to avoid: chase, fate missed us, final goodbye, ended
- Do not overstate: Missing each other is not ending each other.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No dramatic chase-song energy. Keep the mismatch intimate and ordinary.

## Delivery

The song should feel like a near-meeting that keeps arriving a few seconds wrong.

```text
Restrained Korean relationship theme, offset pulse, delayed vocal double, soft ticking percussion, unresolved guitar note, two close voices slightly misaligned. Mood: closing elevator door, opposite crosswalk, cafe threshold, late phone light, near-meetings arriving a few seconds wrong. Avoid chase energy, final goodbye, ended language, fate cliches, and famous song imitation.
```
