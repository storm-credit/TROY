- Song function: Seojun inner monologue

## Sound Direction

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: glass mouth, rain lines, wrong focus, empty center

## Safe Prompt Rule

No hearing-loss effects and no cold-love interpretation.

## Delivery

The song should feel like a known motif drifting into the wrong key without becoming melodramatic.

