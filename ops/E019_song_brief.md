# E019 Song Brief - 이어달리기

## Identity

- Episode: E019
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 같은 속도가 아니어도 한순간 같은 숨을 쉰다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "다른 길을 달려도, 한순간 숨이 겹쳤다."
- Seasonal air: track lane, warm late-spring air, breath after running
- Energy level: mid, forward-moving but gentle

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: light pulse, piano ostinato, airy strings, soft acoustic percussion
- Tempo feel: slow run / steady breath
- Vocal distance: clear, hopeful, still restrained
- Arrangement: pulse should suggest breath, not competition

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: baton, track line, breath, handoff, empty lane after noise
- Point of view: relationship movement, not sports victory
- Words to avoid: victory, finish line as destiny, synchronized souls, forever
- Do not overstate: "동기화" remains emotional metaphor, not canon reveal.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No sports anthem and no named-song references. Build an original track about breath and timing.

## Delivery

The song should carry movement without becoming triumphant.

```text
Gentle Korean campus romance track with forward motion, light pulse, piano ostinato, airy strings, soft acoustic percussion, clear restrained vocal. Mood: two people briefly sharing breath during a relay, timing, handoff, fragile closeness. Lyrics should use baton, track line, breath, handoff, and an empty lane after noise. Avoid victory anthem language, destiny, forever, and explicit synchronization as a supernatural fact.
```
