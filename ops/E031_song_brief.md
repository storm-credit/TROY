# E031 Song Brief - 어색한 기쁨

## Identity

- Episode: E031
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 좋아졌다는 사실보다, 좋아진 뒤의 거리가 더 어색하다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "기쁨도 처음이라, 우리는 조금 삐걱거리며 웃었다."
- Seasonal air: late spring turning early summer, hallway light, post-confession awkward warmth
- Energy level: low-to-mid, shy and warm

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: soft electric piano, light brushed percussion, gentle guitar harmonics, warm room tone
- Tempo feel: small swaying walk
- Vocal distance: close, shy, lightly smiling
- Arrangement: small rhythmic detail to avoid another pure piano ballad

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: two cups remembered, awkward hands, hallway light, careful steps
- Point of view: relationship space with Seojun's self-consciousness
- Words to avoid: perfect, destiny, couple forever, solved
- Do not overstate: This is joy learning how to stand, not stable happiness.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No named songs or artist imitation. Translate awkward warmth and post-confession distance into original texture.

## Delivery

The song should feel like smiling before knowing where to put your hands.

```text
Korean cinematic campus romance theme, soft electric piano, light brushed percussion, gentle guitar harmonics, warm room tone, close shy vocal. Mood: post-confession awkward happiness, careful distance, hallway light, two cups remembered, hands unsure where to rest. Keep it warm but unpracticed. Avoid destiny, forever, perfect love, solved relationship, and triumphant couple-song energy.
```
