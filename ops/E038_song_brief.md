# E038 Song Brief - 둘만의 세계

## Identity

- Episode: E038
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 둘만의 자리는 따뜻했지만, 그만큼 바깥이 흐려졌다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "창가 안쪽은 따뜻했고, 바깥은 조금씩 흐려졌다."
- Seasonal air: cafe routine, warm interior, summer outside blur
- Energy level: mid, warm with slight narrowing

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: cafe electric piano, warm guitar, soft brushed rhythm, light upright bass
- Tempo feel: comfortable loop
- Vocal distance: close and warm, slightly enclosed
- Arrangement: repeated motif should feel cozy but a little circular

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: same seat, two cups, window frame, outside blur, private joke
- Point of view: relationship bubble with faint edge
- Words to avoid: only us forever, destiny room, world disappears, perfect bubble
- Do not overstate: The private world is warm, not sinister.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No imitation. Use cafe loop and window imagery as original emotional texture.

## Delivery

The song should feel cozy, then leave a tiny question mark.

```text
Warm Korean cafe romance theme, cafe electric piano, warm guitar, soft brushed rhythm, light upright bass, close slightly enclosed vocal. Mood: two people building a private routine at the same cafe seat, warm window inside, outside world softly blurred, cozy with a faint edge. Avoid only-us-forever, destiny, perfect bubble, and melodramatic isolation.
```
