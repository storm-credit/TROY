# E005 Song Brief

## episode

- id: E005
- title anchor: 내일을 기다리며
- function: ending reflection
- emotional thesis: 두려운데도 내일을 기다리게 된다

## core feeling

- chorus emotion line: 상처를 기억하는 밤인데도, 나는 자꾸 내일 쪽을 바라봤다
- seasonal air: still late-spring night, cool but not cold, barely moving air
- energy level: low, intimate, bruised but tender

## sound direction

- sonic palette: sparse piano, faint static memory texture, restrained low warmth, wide empty space
- tempo feel: slow and suspended, like lying awake rather than moving forward
- vocal distance: low intimate male vocal, near-whisper, emotionally careful
- arrangement rule: keep the room audible; silence should feel part of the arrangement

## lyrical direction

- dominant image family: bedside lamp, curtain edge, hand on earphones, sleepless room, tomorrow as weak light
- point of view: Seojun interior after the day has ended
- words to avoid: destiny, forever, happy ending, miracle
- plot points not to overstate: do not explain the whole trauma; do not make the song sound cured or secure

## short prose summary

This is the song of a person who still remembers exactly how pain begins, yet finds himself looking toward tomorrow anyway. It should feel like lying awake in a dim room and realizing that fear has not left, but waiting has arrived before permission.

## Suno-ready prompt

```text
Slow Korean reflective ballad, sparse piano, faint static memory texture, intimate low male vocal, quiet night room atmosphere, hand still touching wired earphones, fear of being hurt again but a soft anticipation for tomorrow, emotionally bruised yet tender, cinematic and restrained, no explosive chorus
```

## guardrails

- keep the hope very small and human
- the feeling is not recovery but the beginning of willingness
- leave a soft after-feeling rather than a conclusion
