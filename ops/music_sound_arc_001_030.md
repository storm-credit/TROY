## 4. Control Decision For E031-E040

- E031-E035: awkward warmth, lighter rhythmic details, window/glass textures
- E036-E040: warmer summer texture, more body/air/pulse, but with a faint overheat shadow

Allowed additions:

- soft electric piano
- brushed percussion with more air
- subtle summer room tone
- light bass movement

Still avoid:

- triumphant couple anthem
- overly glossy idol-pop finish
- famous-song imitation language
