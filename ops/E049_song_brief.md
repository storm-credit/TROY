# E049 Song Brief - 친구들의 개입

## Identity

- Episode: E049
- Song function: Ending reflection
- Emotional thesis: 우리만의 세계에도, 바깥의 말들이 문틈으로 들어왔다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "닫아 둔 줄 알았던 문틈으로, 현실의 목소리가 들어왔다."
- Seasonal air: group table, cafe room tone, practical pressure
- Energy level: low-to-mid, grounded

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: cafe room tone, dry guitar, soft bass, subdued percussion, faint group ambience
- Tempo feel: grounded mid-slow
- Vocal distance: close but less dreamy, more awake
- Arrangement: avoid ensemble/comedy bounce

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: group table, cup sound, outside window, practical words, door gap
- Point of view: reflective pressure around the couple
- Words to avoid: friends know best, comic relief, blame, interference
- Do not overstate: Friends are pressure, not the solution.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No sitcom or ensemble-pop energy. Keep it grounded and story-facing.

## Delivery

The song should make outside reality enter quietly.

```text
Grounded Korean ending-reflection track, cafe room tone, dry guitar, soft bass, subdued percussion, faint group ambience, close awake vocal. Mood: practical outside voices entering a private relationship, group table, cup sound, door gap, window to the outside. Avoid comic relief, friends-know-best message, blame, interference drama, and bouncy ensemble energy.
```
