- Chapter: CH04

- Episode logline: 체육대회라는 집단의 소음 속에서 서준은 아린과의 거리가 오히려 더 선명해지는 것을 느낀다.

## Story Module

- Beat purpose: 공연과 방음실 이후의 가까움을 공적 공간으로 옮겨 놓고, 관계가 사적 감각에서 사회적 시선으로 넘어가는 첫 압력을 만든다.
- Scene rhythm: crowd noise -> looking away -> involuntary search -> brief contact across distance -> self-denial.
- Closing turn: 서준은 아린을 보지 않으려 했다고 생각하지만, 그날 가장 많이 기억한 것은 결국 아린이 서 있던 방향이다.

## Music Engine Module

- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional sentence: 사람 많은 곳에서 더 또렷해지는 한 사람.
- Sonic palette: light acoustic guitar, soft piano taps, restrained percussion, warm spring-campus texture.
- Vocal direction: youthful, close but not dramatic, small lift in the chorus.
- Guardrails: Do not make it a victory song or sports anthem. Keep the song about attention and distance, not competition.

## Image Engine Module

- Visual mode: cinematic live-action style
- Key frames: wide shot of the field, Seojun at the edge of the crowd, Arin in a bright but not overexposed pocket of light, water bottle handoff without contact.
- Palette: pale spring yellow, washed grass green, gray-blue campus shadows.
- Continuity rule: Keep Hyedam University field geometry modest and realistic, not stadium-like.

## Video Engine Module

- Recommended cut count: 8
- Sequence: establish field -> crowd texture -> Seojun looking down -> Arin in distance -> cheering interruption -> water bottle close-up -> almost-crossing sightline -> late-afternoon exit.
- Motion rule: Slow lateral pans and held frames; avoid fast sports-edit energy.

## Delivery Gate

- Pre-gen gate: Confirm Arin is foregrounded only through Seojun's perception.
- Post-gen gate: Remove any frame that implies supernatural visibility to others.
- Release gate: Pass if the episode converts public noise into private awareness without confession.
