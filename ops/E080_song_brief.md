# E080 Song Brief - 청취 소실 전환

## Identity

- Episode: E080
- Song function: Ending reflection
- Emotional thesis: 소리는 남아 있었지만, 사랑을 증거로 번역하던 문법이 사라졌다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "세상은 아직 움직였는데, 나는 더 이상 그것을 뜻으로 묶지 못했다."
- Seasonal air: mouth without subtitles, dust in light, open window
- Energy level: low, open, unresolved

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: interpretation collapse, missing grammar, pitch-blurred memory, open unresolved air
- Tempo feel: grammarless drift
- Vocal distance: close at first, then widening into room
- Arrangement: leave sound present but remove its old emotional syntax

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: moving mouth, no subtitles, hand gesture, dust in light, open frame
- Point of view: Seojun at the threshold of listening loss
- Words to avoid: mature silence, complete peace, ability returned, love disappeared
- Do not overstate: This is not `completion of silence` yet.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No magical silence, no despair ending, no famous art-pop imitation.

## Delivery

The song should create a blank that Phase V can enter, not close the story.

```text
Minimal Korean interpretation-collapse reflection track, missing grammar, pitch-blurred memory, open unresolved air, sound present but emotionally unreadable, close vocal widening into room. Mood: mouth moving without subtitles, hand gesture, dust in light, open window, a world still moving while old emotional syntax disappears. Avoid mature-silence language, complete peace, ability-returned lyrics, love-disappeared framing, despair ending, and famous song imitation.
```
