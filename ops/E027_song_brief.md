# E027 Song Brief - 망설임

## Identity

- Episode: E027
- Song function: Seojun inner monologue
- Emotional thesis: 확신을 훔치고 싶을수록, 진짜 말은 어려워진다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "알고 싶은 마음이 깊어질수록, 말할 용기는 더 멀어졌다."
- Seasonal air: stair landing, open window, erased sentence
- Energy level: low, tense, withheld

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: muted piano, held strings, quiet pulse, low room tone
- Tempo feel: suspended and hesitant
- Vocal distance: close, conflicted, fragile
- Arrangement: tension without release

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: stair landing, erased line, open window, stopped foot
- Point of view: Seojun resisting the temptation to over-read
- Words to avoid: moral lecture, power, manipulate, steal as literal crime
- Do not overstate: The conflict should feel emotional and ethical, not procedural.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No reference to any existing song. Keep the tension original and small.

## Delivery

The song should feel like standing on a stair landing and not knowing whether to go up or down.

```text
Tense restrained Korean inner monologue, muted piano, held strings, quiet pulse, low room tone, close conflicted vocal. Mood: hesitation before confession, wanting certainty but knowing it cannot replace speech, stair landing, erased sentence, open window, stopped foot. Avoid moral lecture, secret-power explanation, dramatic villain language, and big release chorus.
```
