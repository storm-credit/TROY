# E104 Song Brief - 말로 묻기

## Identity

- Episode: E104
- Song function: Seojun inner monologue
- Emotional thesis: 사랑은 알아듣는 능력이 아니라, 끝까지 묻는 용기로 다시 시작됐다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "이번엔 알아듣는 대신, 끝까지 묻고 남겨둘 자리를 배웠다."
- Seasonal air: 컵 물기, 기다리는 정적, 낮은 카페 조명, 멈췄다가 나오는 질문
- Energy level: low-medium, focused, exposed

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: sparse piano gaps, cold pad, dry breath noise, slow question pulse
- Tempo feel: hesitant question-and-answer
- Vocal distance: close, restrained tremor
- Arrangement: pause before each important line; do not reward the question with a swell

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: wet cup ring, waiting face, unfinished sentence, correction landing
- Point of view: Seojun choosing language over interpretation
- Words to avoid: telepathy, instant absolution, full apology montage, healed silence
- Do not overstate: The question opens a method, not closure.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No sermon tone, no apology crescendo, no triumphant understanding cue.

## Delivery

The song should feel like holding the space after a hard question instead of rushing to solve it.

```text
Korean introspective late-autumn track, sparse piano gaps, cold pad, dry breath noise, close restrained male vocal. Mood: wet cup ring, waiting silence, a rehearsed question finally spoken, correction heard without defense, language replacing interpretation. Avoid telepathy imagery, instant absolution, healed-silence framing, dramatic apology lift, and famous song imitation.
```
