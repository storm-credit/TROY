# E039 Song Brief - 안정의 표면

## Identity

- Episode: E039
- Song function: Ending reflection
- Emotional thesis: 안정은 때로 가장 조용한 오해의 이름이었다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "아무 일도 없던 날들이, 가장 얇게 금이 가고 있었다."
- Seasonal air: repeated cafe seat, warm surface, thin line of light
- Energy level: low, calm with shadow

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: soft electric piano, dry guitar, low sustained note, minimal brushed percussion
- Tempo feel: slow and steady, almost too smooth
- Vocal distance: close, calm, slightly shadowed
- Arrangement: keep the surface pretty while the low note suggests unease

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: repeated cup, closed notebook, thin light, same seat, smooth surface
- Point of view: reflective foreshadowing
- Words to avoid: crack as obvious disaster, doom, inevitable breakup, betrayal
- Do not overstate: Foreshadow gently.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No melodramatic breakup-song imitation. Keep it restrained and surface-level.

## Delivery

The song should sound calm enough that the unease arrives late.

```text
Restrained Korean ending-reflection track, soft electric piano, dry guitar, low sustained note, minimal brushed percussion, close calm vocal with slight shadow. Mood: relationship stability that feels almost too smooth, repeated cup, closed notebook, same seat, thin line of light. Avoid obvious doom, breakup prediction, betrayal language, and melodramatic climax.
```
