## Identity

- Episode: E075
- Song function: Seojun inner monologue

- Chorus emotion line: "나는 들었다고 믿었고, 그래서 묻는 법을 잊고 있었다."
- Seasonal air: edge of frame, late notification, notebook margin

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: pitch-blurred memory, missing overtone, thin ticking room, half-muted motif
- Tempo feel: slow self-recognition
- Vocal distance: close, bare, not triumphant
- Arrangement: do not resolve the self-awareness into growth yet

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: out-of-focus object, late alert, passing hand, notebook margin
- Point of view: Seojun seeing his evidence habit

## Safe Prompt Rule

No self-help clarity. Keep the realization painful and unfinished.
