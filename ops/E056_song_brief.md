# E056 Song Brief - 말하지 않은 것들

## Identity

- Episode: E056
- Song function: Seojun inner monologue
- Emotional thesis: 말하지 않은 것들은 사라지지 않고, 조용히 자리를 차지했다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "묻지 않은 말들이, 우리 사이에 먼저 앉아 있었다."
- Seasonal air: window silence, folded paper, heavy afternoon
- Energy level: low, heavy but controlled

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: dry piano, paper texture, low room tone, faint pulse
- Tempo feel: slow and weighted
- Vocal distance: close, regretful but not fully broken
- Arrangement: leave silence after chorus phrases

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: folded paper, closed lips, chair gap, late answer
- Point of view: Seojun recognizing what he did not ask
- Words to avoid: accusation, fault, villain, over-explanation
- Do not overstate: No breakup tone yet.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No dramatic blame-song imitation. Keep it human and restrained.

## Delivery

The song should feel like unasked questions taking up physical space.

```text
Quiet Korean inner monologue ballad, dry piano, subtle paper texture, low room tone, faint pulse, close regretful vocal. Mood: realizing unasked questions have taken up space between two people, folded paper, closed lips, chair gap, late answer, heavy afternoon. Avoid accusation, villain language, breakup climax, and direct explanation.
```
