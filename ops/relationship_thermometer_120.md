# Relationship Thermometer: 120 Episodes

## 1. Purpose

각 화가 끝날 때 관계가 실제로 1mm라도 이동했는지 확인한다.

이 문서는 사건 기록이 아니라 관계 이동 기록이다.

## 2. Allowed States

- closer
- farther
- stalled
- distorted closeness
- redefined

## 3. Core Rule

관계를 바꾸는 것은 사건이 아니라 선택이다.

우선 기록할 선택:

- asked
- answered
- withheld
- avoided
- stayed
- stepped back

## 4. Main Couple Baseline

### Seojun -> Arin

- start state: curiosity mixed with dependency
- middle risk: listening faster than asking
- ending state: understanding without proof

### Arin -> Seojun

- start state: ease and interest
- middle risk: feeling pre-read and cornered
- ending state: self-respecting love that can still leave

## 5. Phase Anchors

- Phase 1: curiosity becomes orientation
- Phase 2: orientation becomes mutual rhythm
- Phase 3: rhythm becomes pressure
- Phase 4: pressure becomes fracture
- Phase 5: fracture becomes reinterpretation
- Phase 6: reinterpretation becomes farewell with truth
