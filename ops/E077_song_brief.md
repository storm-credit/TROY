# E077 Song Brief - 서로 다른 언어

## Identity

- Episode: E077
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 같은 사랑을 말했지만, 서로 다른 박자에서 끝났다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "우리는 같은 마음을 말했는데, 서로 다른 언어로 아팠다."
- Seasonal air: same table, two cups, half-shadow, small phone barrier
- Energy level: mid-low, tense, articulate

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: misaligned duet timing, separate breath points, withheld refrain, split piano and guitar phrases
- Tempo feel: two emotional meters in one room
- Vocal distance: two near voices that never fully blend
- Arrangement: avoid harmony resolution

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: two cups, table line, half-shadow, phone as barrier
- Point of view: two languages of love failing translation
- Words to avoid: villain, blame, winner, loser, final proof
- Do not overstate: Neither character is wrong enough to simplify.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No resolved duet harmony and no blame-ballad framing.

## Delivery

The song should let two truths coexist without landing in the same meter.

```text
Intimate Korean relationship dialogue theme, misaligned duet timing, separate breath points, withheld refrain, split piano and guitar phrases, two near voices that never fully blend. Mood: same table, two cups, half-shadow, phone as small barrier, two languages of love failing translation.
```
