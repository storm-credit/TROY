# E020 Song Brief - 뒤풀이

## Identity

- Episode: E020
- Song function: Ending reflection
- Emotional thesis: 하루가 끝난 뒤에도 어떤 사람의 자리만 남는다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "소란이 걷힌 자리에, 네가 앉았던 빛만 남았다."
- Seasonal air: warm indoor yellow fading into cool night
- Energy level: low, reflective, phase-closing

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: late-evening acoustic ballad, soft piano, faint room ambience, gentle guitar
- Tempo feel: slow afterparty walk home
- Vocal distance: restrained and warm, slightly tired
- Arrangement: no dramatic climax; close with unresolved possibility

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: paper cup, long table, hallway light, night gate, remaining laughter
- Point of view: Seojun after the group noise fades
- Words to avoid: confession, promise, forever, destiny, couple
- Do not overstate: This closes Phase 1 with an open door, not a relationship declaration.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No famous ballad imitation. Translate late-evening afterglow into original melody and imagery.

## Delivery

The song should feel like the first movement closing with a door left open.

```text
Late-evening Korean acoustic ballad for a campus romance afterparty, soft piano, gentle guitar, faint room ambience, restrained warm vocal, slow walking-home tempo. Mood: after the noise fades, one person's seat and light remain, open possibility, no confession yet. Lyrics should use paper cup, long table, hallway light, night gate, and remaining laughter. Avoid forever, destiny, promise, couple language, and dramatic climax.
```
