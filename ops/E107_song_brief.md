# E107 Song Brief - 예외의 의미

## Identity

- Episode: E107
- Song function: Seojun inner monologue
- Emotional thesis: 특별했던 건 사람이 아니라, 그 사람 앞에서 내가 끝내지 못했던 방식이었다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "특별했던 건 네가 아니라, 너 앞에서 내가 끝내지 못한 방식이었다."
- Seasonal air: 늦가을 창 그림자, 조용한 테이블, 고개 숙였다 드는 숨, 바로 고치지 않는 대답
- Energy level: low, lucid, pressed

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: dry piano intervals, soft room hum, thin low string line, delayed breath
- Tempo feel: careful processing after a structural truth
- Vocal distance: close, reflective
- Arrangement: keep revelation quiet and human, not mystical

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: shadowed window, lowered gaze, shared authorship, structure instead of magic
- Point of view: Seojun reclassifying exception as relational truth
- Words to avoid: soulmate, chosen one, destiny, ability unlocked
- Do not overstate: Exception is relational, not supernatural privilege.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No mystical shimmer, no fated-bond chorus, no power-return cue.

## Delivery

The song should land like a quiet correction that changes the whole map.

```text
Korean introspective relationship-correction track, dry piano intervals, soft room hum, thin low string line, close reflective vocal. Mood: late-autumn window shadow, a quiet table conversation where exception is redefined as something two people made together, structural truth heavier than romance. Avoid soulmate language, chosen-one framing, ability-return imagery, mystical shimmer, and famous song imitation.
```
