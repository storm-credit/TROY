# E073 Song Brief - 어색한 친절

## Identity

- Episode: E073
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 다정함은 남았는데, 그 다정함이 더 어색했다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "착하려고 내민 손이, 예전보다 더 멀게 닿았다."
- Seasonal air: borrowed umbrella, held door, untouched drink
- Energy level: low, tender but misaligned

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: incomplete chord repeat, delayed breath, soft misaligned guitar, thin pad
- Tempo feel: careful kindness out of sync
- Vocal distance: close but not blended
- Arrangement: keep guitar and vocal slightly offset

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: umbrella handoff, door held open, drink left behind, missed eye contact
- Point of view: kindness without ease
- Words to avoid: sweet couple, saved us, perfect kindness, healed
- Do not overstate: Kindness is effort, not resolution.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No sweet montage energy. Keep tenderness awkward.

## Delivery

The song should make kindness feel sincere but unable to restore comfort.

```text
Quiet Korean relationship theme, incomplete chord repeat, delayed breath, soft misaligned guitar, thin pad, close but unblended vocal. Mood: umbrella handoff, held door, untouched drink, missed eye contact, sincere kindness that cannot restore comfort. Avoid sweet couple montage, healed language, perfect-kindness cliches, and famous song imitation.
```
