# E093 Song Brief - 편안함과 사랑

## Identity

- Episode: E093
- Song function: Ending reflection
- Emotional thesis: 덜 불편한 관계가 반드시 더 사랑인 것은 아니었다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "편안하다고 해서, 그게 끝내 사랑인 것은 아니었다."
- Seasonal air: 테이블 위 컵, 멈춘 입술, 대답 뒤의 빈 공간
- Energy level: low, clear, unsentimental

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: low wood-tone percussion, clipped soft pad, short keyboard decay, centerless tempo
- Tempo feel: hesitant and slightly off-center
- Vocal distance: medium-close, flat but not cold
- Arrangement: keep the floor narrow; avoid emotional cushioning

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: cup, hand, table edge, empty seatline
- Point of view: separating comfort from truth
- Words to avoid: safe love, home, finally right, easier equals better
- Do not overstate: Do not turn Yu-bin into the ideal answer or Arin into a mistake.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No warm duet feel, no “homecoming” lyric field, no soft closure cadence.

## Delivery

The song should leave behind the realization that emotional difficulty and emotional truth are not the same scale.

```text
Quiet Korean autumn reflection track, low wood-tone percussion, clipped soft pad, short keyboard decay, centerless tempo, medium-close plain vocal. Mood: cup on table edge, paused lips, empty seatline, answer followed by more space than comfort. Avoid safe-love slogans, homecoming mood, easier-equals-better framing, soft closure cadence, and famous song imitation.
```
