# E067 Song Brief - 상처의 언어

## Identity

- Episode: E067
- Song function: Relationship rupture theme
- Emotional thesis: 맞는 말도 묻지 않고 건네면 상처가 된다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "네가 맞춘 마음이, 내게는 묻지 않은 상처였다."
- Seasonal air: dry light inside damp season, deleted text, crushed paper cup
- Energy level: mid, sharp but contained

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: stripped background, dry vocal edge, erased percussion layer, short reverb cut
- Tempo feel: tense spoken-edge pulse
- Vocal distance: close, uncomfortably clear
- Arrangement: remove layers so words feel exposed

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: typed-deleted message, paper cup, closed laptop, turned jawline
- Point of view: two true feelings spoken badly
- Words to avoid: blame anthem, villain, never loved me, final verdict
- Do not overstate: These words wound, but they do not officially end the relationship.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No melodramatic crying chorus or revenge-ballad tone.

## Delivery

The song should make listening feel like trespass when it replaces asking.

```text
Contained Korean relationship-rupture theme, stripped background, dry vocal edge, erased percussion layer, short reverb cut, close uncomfortable vocal. Mood: typed-deleted message, crushed paper cup, closed laptop, turned jawline, true words spoken in the worst way. Avoid blame anthem, villain framing, never-loved-me lyrics, final verdict, melodramatic crying chorus, and famous song imitation.
```
