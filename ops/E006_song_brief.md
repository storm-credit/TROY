# E006 Song Brief

## episode

- id: E006
- title anchor: 문학과 철학
- function: relationship theme
- emotional thesis: 소통과 이해는 같은 말이 아니다

## core feeling

- chorus emotion line: 같은 문장을 읽고도, 우리는 다른 온도로 이해하고 있었다
- seasonal air: late-spring afternoon, warm classroom light with soft air
- energy level: calm, thoughtful, quietly opening

## sound direction

- sonic palette: acoustic guitar, piano, light duet feel, restrained rhythm
- tempo feel: gentle mid-slow conversational flow
- vocal distance: close and human, less solitary than prior tracks
- arrangement rule: keep the song warm but not triumphant; the rhythm should feel like back-and-forth dialogue

## lyrical direction

- dominant image family: notebook edges, sunlight on desks, shared pages, different temperatures in the same room
- point of view: relational perspective, still close to Seojun but with room for a shared emotional field
- words to avoid: fate, soulmate, obvious confession, abstract philosophy jargon
- plot points not to overstate: do not flatten the song into classroom recap; do not pretend they fully understand each other yet

## short prose summary

This is the song of two people who can stay inside the same conversation long enough to feel close, while still missing part of what the other really means. It should feel like sunlight landing on the same wooden desk, but warming each hand a little differently.

```text
Korean conversational relationship theme, acoustic guitar and piano, warm but restrained vocal, classroom afternoon sunlight, notebook margins and shared pages, theme of communication and misunderstanding, literary campus mood, gentle human rhythm, emotionally thoughtful, no grand confession, cinematic and intimate
```

## guardrails

- the warmth should come from dialogue, not from certainty
- keep the song grounded in ordinary campus detail
- closeness must remain slightly incomplete
