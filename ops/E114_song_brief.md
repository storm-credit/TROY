# E114 Song Brief - 마지막 시험

## Identity

- Episode: E114
- Song function: Seojun inner monologue
- Emotional thesis: 시험은 돌아가지 않는 데 있다

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "왜 그랬어 대신 괜찮아?를 남기는 연습"
- Seasonal air: 뒤집힌 휴대폰, 지워지는 문장, 낮은 실내광, 멈춘 손
- Energy level: low, taut, controlled

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: sparse piano, clipped breath, low room hum, restrained unresolved tail
- Tempo feel: restraint under pressure
- Vocal distance: medium-close, internal but steady
- Arrangement: keep the tension small and precise

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: deleted text, question replacing accusation, paused impulse
- Point of view: Seojun interrupting his own suspicion loop
- Words to avoid: jealousy, accusation, certainty, punishment
- Do not overstate: The test is refusal to return, not victory.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No suspicion anthem, no anger release, no certainty lock.

## Delivery

The song should feel like a hand stopping before it turns into an old habit.

```text
Korean restrained inner monologue track, sparse piano, clipped breath, low room hum, restrained unresolved tail, medium-close internal vocal. Mood: an upside-down phone, a deleted message, low indoor light, and the act of replacing accusation with a single caring question. Avoid jealousy framing, accusation hooks, certainty language, punishment tone, and famous song imitation.
```
