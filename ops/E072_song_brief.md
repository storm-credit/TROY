# E072 Song Brief - 재시도

## Identity

- Episode: E072
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 다시 말하려는 마음은 있었지만, 문장은 같은 곳에서 젖었다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "다시 시작하려던 말이, 같은 자리에서 식어갔다."
- Seasonal air: Cafe Dal warmth, cup not yet cold, half-folded memo
- Energy level: low-to-mid, briefly warmer, unresolved

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: sparse piano, warm thin room tone, delayed vocal entry, unresolved loop
- Tempo feel: hesitant restart
- Vocal distance: close, trying to soften
- Arrangement: small warmth enters, then fails to settle

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: warm cup, reopened chat, half-folded memo, cooling table light
- Point of view: two people attempting repair
- Words to avoid: reconciliation, healed, back to us, miracle
- Do not overstate: The attempt should reveal the failure.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No reconciliation anthem and no direct famous song reference.

## Delivery

The song should briefly warm the room, then let the same unresolved silence return.

```text
Intimate Korean relationship-repair attempt track, sparse piano, warm but thin room tone, delayed vocal entry, unresolved loop, close soft vocal. Mood: Cafe Dal warmth, cup not yet cold, reopened chat, half-folded memo, repair attempt that cannot settle. Avoid reconciliation anthem, healed language, miracle lyrics, glossy couple energy, and famous song imitation.
```
