# E111 Song Brief - 선택의 시작

## Identity

- Episode: E111
- Song function: Seojun inner monologue
- Emotional thesis: 증거를 찾지 않는 선택이 결말의 바닥이 된다

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "증거를 찾지 말 것, 오늘의 공기를 그냥 통과할 것"
- Seasonal air: 창가, 짧아진 햇빛, 구겨졌다 펴지는 이어폰, 노트 한 줄
- Energy level: low, inward, resolved

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: restrained piano, thin breath noise, faint room air, unforced low tone
- Tempo feel: quiet decision-making pulse
- Vocal distance: medium-close, close internal voice
- Arrangement: keep the line spare and unsentimental

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: window light, folded earphones, single line of text
- Point of view: Seojun interrupting his own proof-seeking habit
- Words to avoid: destiny, certainty, reunion, triumph
- Do not overstate: This is a beginning of choice, not a final answer.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No proof-chase chorus, no certainty build, no comeback glow.

## Delivery

The song should feel like choosing not to chase a conclusion.

```text
Korean introspective track, restrained piano, thin breath noise, faint room air, unforced low tone, medium-close internal vocal. Mood: window light, folded earphones, a notebook with one line, choosing to stop proof-seeking and let the day pass through. Avoid destiny language, certainty lift, reunion framing, triumphal chorus, and famous song imitation.
```
