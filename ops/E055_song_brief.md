# E055 Song Brief - 금의 시작

## Identity

- Episode: E055
- Song function: Ending reflection
- Emotional thesis: 너무 밝은 빛 아래에서, 금은 더 늦게 보였다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "금은 소리 없이 생겨서, 우리는 한참 늦게 보았다."
- Seasonal air: after-brightness, thin light line, late reply
- Energy level: low, subtle unease

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: warm pad, low shadow note, dry guitar, minimal brushed percussion
- Tempo feel: slow settling pulse
- Vocal distance: close, reflective, slightly dimmed
- Arrangement: no rupture; just one low note under the warmth

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: line of light, late phone glow, untouched cup, thin crack
- Point of view: retrospective reflection
- Words to avoid: breakup, doom, shattered, betrayal
- Do not overstate: This is a first hairline crack.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No melodramatic breakup imitation. Keep the crack almost invisible.

## Delivery

The song should sound like noticing something only after the room gets quiet.

```text
Restrained Korean ending-reflection track, warm pad, low shadow note, dry guitar, minimal brushed percussion, close slightly dimmed vocal. Mood: the first hairline crack after a very bright day, line of light, late phone glow, untouched cup, quiet unease. Avoid breakup, doom, shattered, betrayal, and melodramatic climax.
```
