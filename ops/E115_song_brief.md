# E115 Song Brief - 증명

## Identity

- Episode: E115
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 진심은 붙잡지 않아도 인정할 수 있다

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "우리 사랑이 없었던 건 아니지, 응 진짜였어"
- Seasonal air: 캠퍼스 계단, 가까워지지 않은 거리, 고마워
- Energy level: low, clear, dignified

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: sparse piano, restrained strings, soft low sustain, air-forward ending
- Tempo feel: still and approving
- Vocal distance: medium-close, steady
- Arrangement: keep the approval understated, not finale-like

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: 계단, 승인, 손의 거리, 공백
- Point of view: mutual truth without ownership
- Words to avoid: reunion, forever, closure, last embrace
- Do not overstate: This is proof through release, not through possession.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No reunion declaration, no melodrama, no finale glow.

## Delivery

The song should feel like an answer that leaves room for the other person to remain themselves.

```text
Korean dignified relationship track, sparse piano, restrained strings, soft low sustain, air-forward ending, medium-close steady vocal. Mood: campus steps, a little distance between two people, gratitude that does not turn into possession, and a truth that can remain without being held. Avoid reunion language, forever promises, closure tone, last-embrace imagery, and famous song imitation.
```
