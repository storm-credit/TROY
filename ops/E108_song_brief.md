# E108 Song Brief - 들리지 않아도

## Identity

- Episode: E108
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 확신은 줄어들었지만, 태도는 더 분명해졌다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "들리지 않는 자리에 남은 건, 손을 빼지 않고 물어보는 쪽의 태도였다."
- Seasonal air: 늦가을 보행로, 평행한 걸음, `그게 정상이에요`라는 작은 웃음, 불안의 고백
- Energy level: low, humane, lightly warmed

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: open piano, light ambient field, soft pulse, clean unresolved tail
- Tempo feel: patient walking pace
- Vocal distance: medium-close, steadier
- Arrangement: let uncertainty remain audible; do not brighten it into certainty

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: parallel walk, question left open, normal uncertainty, staying anyway
- Point of view: two people learning that proof is not the same as trust
- Words to avoid: certainty, hearing regained, now I know, miracle
- Do not overstate: Normal uncertainty is the achievement.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No certainty anthem, no magical reassurance, no glowing-lock chorus.

## Delivery

The song should feel like walking beside someone without needing to know everything.

```text
Korean relationship track with open piano, light ambient field, soft pulse, clean unresolved tail, medium-close steady vocal. Mood: parallel late-autumn walk, admitting uncertainty, being told that uncertainty is normal, choosing attitude over proof. Avoid certainty language, hearing-return cues, miracle reassurance, bright romantic lock-in, and famous song imitation.
```
