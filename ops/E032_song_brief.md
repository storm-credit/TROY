# E032 Song Brief - 서로의 속도

## Identity

- Episode: E032
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 같은 길을 가려면 같은 속도부터 강요하지 않아야 한다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "네가 늦은 게 아니라, 내가 먼저 걸어간 거였다."
- Seasonal air: early summer crosswalk, slow afternoon, softened street noise
- Energy level: low-to-mid, reflective with gentle movement

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: soft electric piano pulse, nylon guitar, brushed percussion, light bass movement
- Tempo feel: walking tempo with tiny pauses
- Vocal distance: close but steadier than E031
- Arrangement: use small rhythmic stops to mirror pace adjustment

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: crosswalk, half-step ahead, shoes slowing, long shadow
- Point of view: Seojun learning to wait
- Words to avoid: match perfectly, one speed, destiny, blame
- Do not overstate: The speed difference is a small relational lesson, not a breakup.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No imitation of existing city-pop or romance songs. Use pace and street-air imagery only.

## Delivery

The song should feel like learning to slow down without calling it sacrifice.

```text
Restrained Korean campus romance track, soft electric piano pulse, nylon guitar, brushed percussion, light bass movement, close steady vocal. Mood: learning another person's pace, crosswalk pause, half-step ahead, shoes slowing, long afternoon shadow. Lyrics should feel reflective and warm, not blaming. Avoid destiny, perfect match, one-speed love, and dramatic conflict language.
```
