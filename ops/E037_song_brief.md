# E037 Song Brief - 가까운 행복

## Identity

- Episode: E037
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 가장 가까운 행복은 너무 조용해서 오래갈 것처럼 보인다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "조용해서, 오래갈 줄 알았다."
- Seasonal air: early summer campus date, iced drink, warm bench light
- Energy level: mid-light, sweet but grounded

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: warm acoustic pop, light bass, brushed percussion, soft electric piano accents
- Tempo feel: relaxed summer walk
- Vocal distance: relaxed and close
- Arrangement: simple hook, no glossy overproduction

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: iced cup, bench, summer tree shade, hands near but not claimed
- Point of view: relationship happiness with later-loss residue
- Words to avoid: perfect day, forever, never-ending, destiny
- Do not overstate: Happiness must stay ordinary.

## Safe Prompt Rule

Avoid famous summer-pop imitation. Build original ordinary happiness.

## Delivery

The song should be sweet enough to be missed later.

```text
Grounded Korean summer campus romance song, warm acoustic pop texture, light bass, brushed percussion, soft electric piano accents, relaxed close vocal. Mood: simple nearby happiness, iced cup, bench, summer tree shade, hands almost touching, quiet enough to seem lasting. Avoid perfect day, forever, destiny, never-ending, and glossy idol-pop production.
```
