# E057 Song Brief - 다르게 느끼는 사랑

## Identity

- Episode: E057
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 같은 마음이라 믿었지만, 우리는 다른 언어로 따뜻했다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "우리는 둘 다 따뜻했지만, 서로 다른 쪽에서 타고 있었다."
- Seasonal air: same table, different eye-lines, warm light with cool edge
- Energy level: low-to-mid, sad but tender

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: two-texture arrangement, warm electric piano against dry guitar, soft bass
- Tempo feel: slow relational sway
- Vocal distance: close, balanced, not accusatory
- Arrangement: let two instrumental colors coexist without fully blending

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: different gestures, same table, missed timing, two profiles
- Point of view: relationship difference, both sincere
- Words to avoid: wrong love, blame, incompatible forever, betrayal
- Do not overstate: Difference is painful but not evil.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No breakup duet imitation. Keep both sides sympathetic.

## Delivery

The song should sound like two warm lights that do not quite overlap.

```text
Tender Korean relationship theme, warm electric piano contrasted with dry guitar, soft bass, minimal percussion, close balanced vocal. Mood: two people both loving sincerely but feeling love in different languages, same table, different eye-lines, missed timing, two profiles. Avoid blame, wrong-love, betrayal, incompatible-forever, and melodramatic breakup framing.
```
