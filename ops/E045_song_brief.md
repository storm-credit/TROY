# E045 Song Brief - 흔들리는 해석

## Identity

- Episode: E045
- Song function: Seojun inner monologue
- Emotional thesis: 더 들으려 할수록, 네 말은 더 멀어졌다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "붙잡은 건 소리였고, 놓친 건 네 말이었다."
- Seasonal air: blurred glass, crowded room tone, over-focused anxiety
- Energy level: low-to-mid, tense and unstable

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: layered low pulse, glassy piano, tense room tone, clipped percussion
- Tempo feel: uneven internal pulse
- Vocal distance: close but pressured
- Arrangement: overlapping textures should feel like over-reading, not magic

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: blurred reflection, closed notebook, crowded noise, hand tension
- Point of view: Seojun's flawed interpretation loop
- Words to avoid: power-up, mind reading, truth signal, guaranteed
- Do not overstate: Over-reading is a flaw, not a skill upgrade.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No supernatural effect prompt and no named-song imitation.

## Delivery

The song should make interpretation feel less reliable, not more powerful.

```text
Tense Korean psychological romance track, layered low pulse, glassy piano, tense room tone, clipped percussion, close pressured vocal. Mood: trying to hear more and understanding less, blurred reflection, closed notebook, crowded noise, hand tension. Avoid mind-reading language, power-up feeling, truth-signal lyrics, and supernatural sound effects.
```
