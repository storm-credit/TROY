# E059 Song Brief - 쌓이는 피로

## Identity

- Episode: E059
- Song function: Ending reflection
- Emotional thesis: 사랑은 남아 있었고, 그래서 더 지쳤다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "아직 좋아해서, 우리는 더 오래 지쳤다."
- Seasonal air: late phone light, closed cafe chair, heavy night air
- Energy level: low, tired, restrained

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: low pad, slow bass, dry piano, tired room ambience
- Tempo feel: slow drag
- Vocal distance: close but depleted
- Arrangement: no dramatic fall; let repetition feel exhausting

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: tired shoulders, late phone, untouched cup, night air
- Point of view: mutual fatigue, not one-sided blame
- Words to avoid: hate, no love left, final goodbye, collapse
- Do not overstate: The relationship is not over yet.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No full breakup ballad. Keep love present under fatigue.

## Delivery

The song should feel like staying because love remains, and tiring because staying hurts.

```text
Tired Korean ending-reflection ballad, low pad, slow bass, dry piano, muted room ambience, close depleted vocal. Mood: mutual fatigue inside a relationship where love still remains, late phone light, tired shoulders, untouched cup, heavy night air. Avoid hate, no-love-left, final goodbye, collapse, and full breakup-ballad climax.
```
