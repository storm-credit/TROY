## Cut List

1. Close conversation: Seojun and Arin seated or standing close, ordinary light.
2. Arin turns slightly: Arin looking away, not cold, just inward.
3. Glass reflection: window glass shows Seojun's reflection instead of direct access.
4. Seojun tries to read: restrained close-up, anxiety beginning.
5. Blurred light: light on glass becomes soft and distant.
6. Hands not moving: hands on table or at sides, no contact.
7. Window line: a vertical glass line divides the composition.
8. Widened distance: same room, more space visible between them.
9. Unanswered face: Arin's expression protected, not decoded.
10. Corridor after: quiet corridor after the moment, muted light.
