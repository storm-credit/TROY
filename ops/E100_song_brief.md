# E100 Song Brief - 문턱

## Identity

- Episode: E100
- Song function: Ending reflection
- Emotional thesis: 다시 시작이 아니라, 이전과 같은 방식으로는 더 이상 서 있을 수 없다는 확인이다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "문턱 앞에 선 건 희망이 아니라, 다른 태도로만 다음을 건널 수 있다는 사실이었다."
- Seasonal air: 문손잡이, 계단선, 흐린 아침, 열리지 않은 다음 프레임
- Energy level: low, poised, unresolved

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: thin open harmony, delayed low-end release, restrained impact, unresolved tail
- Tempo feel: one small step, no arrival
- Vocal distance: medium-close, clearer but still unclosed
- Arrangement: finish with an open edge, not a statement

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: threshold, handle, stair line, cloudy morning, paused hand
- Point of view: standing before change without announcing rebirth
- Words to avoid: new start, destiny restart, hearing returned, completed silence
- Do not overstate: Episode 100 is a hinge, not a payoff.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No commemorative ballad, no recovered motif clarity, no big hopeful turn.

## Delivery

The song should close the 100th episode like a door not yet opened, not like a curtain call.

```text
Open Korean autumn hinge track, thin open harmony, delayed low-end release, restrained impact, unresolved tail, medium-close clear but unclosed vocal. Mood: hand near a handle, stair line, cloudy morning, threshold not yet crossed, one small step without rebirth. Avoid new-start framing, destiny restart, hearing-return cues, completed-silence language, big hopeful turn, and famous song imitation.
```
