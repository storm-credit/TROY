# E079 Song Brief - 이후

## Identity

- Episode: E079
- Song function: Ending reflection
- Emotional thesis: 끝난 사건보다 무서운 건, 아무 일 없다는 듯 이어지는 하루였다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "하루는 계속됐고, 그게 제일 조용하게 무너졌다."
- Seasonal air: empty class seat, alone at Cafe Dal, wider corridor
- Energy level: low, numb, ordinary

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: unstable daily rhythm, dim room pulse, pitch-blurred memory, near-silent vocal tail
- Tempo feel: walking rhythm that cannot settle
- Vocal distance: distant-close, like an empty room
- Arrangement: keep the world moving while the inner meter fails

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: empty adjacent seat, unsaved draft, face-down phone, wide corridor
- Point of view: after breakup, no renegotiation
- Words to avoid: come back, new love, mourning anthem, life ended
- Do not overstate: This is everyday dysfunction, not theatrical collapse.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No reunion tease and no grief-anthem imitation.

## Delivery

The song should make ordinary campus life feel slightly unusable.

```text
Quiet Korean after-breakup reflection track, unstable daily rhythm, dim room pulse, pitch-blurred memory, near-silent vocal tail, distant-close empty-room vocal. Mood: empty adjacent class seat, alone at Cafe Dal, unsaved draft, face-down phone, wider corridor, ordinary life continuing while the inner meter fails. Avoid reunion tease, come-back lyrics, new-love hints, grief anthem, life-ended melodrama, and famous song imitation.
```
