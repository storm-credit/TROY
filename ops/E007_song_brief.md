# E007 Song Brief

## episode

- id: E007
- title anchor: 같은 조가 되다
- function: relationship theme
- emotional thesis: 피할 수 없는 가까움이 시작된다

## core feeling

- chorus emotion line: 멀리 두려던 사람이, 어느새 같은 종이 위에 이름을 올렸다
- seasonal air: late-spring daytime, bright but soft classroom air
- energy level: fresh, slightly awkward, gently tightening

## sound direction

- sonic palette: mid-tempo acoustic pop with soft piano, light campus pulse, restrained warmth
- tempo feel: lightly moving, practical and human rather than dreamy
- vocal distance: close relational vocal with a small amount of shared lift
- arrangement rule: let the rhythm suggest proximity by routine, not confession or sweep

## lyrical direction

- dominant image family: same paper, pulled chairs, shared desk edge, names on one page
- point of view: relational perspective leaning toward Seojun's reluctant awareness
- words to avoid: soulmate, destiny, bubbly rom-com phrasing, obvious love confession
- plot points not to overstate: do not make the assignment itself the story; focus on the after-feeling of unavoidable closeness

## short prose summary

This is the song of two people who have been given a practical excuse to keep meeting, and who both understand that the excuse matters less than the fact that it feels relieving. The song should feel like chairs being pulled closer than intended.

## Suno-ready prompt

```text
Korean intimate relationship theme, mid-tempo acoustic pop with soft piano, warm but restrained vocal, shared desk and project handout mood, late-spring campus classroom air, awkward closeness becoming natural, fresh and human, not bubbly, no direct confession, cinematic and lightly tense
```

## guardrails

- the song should feel more practical than dreamy on the surface
- do not glamorize the setup into fate
- keep the emotional job singular: structured proximity becoming quiet relief
