# E069 Song Brief - 파열 이후

## Identity

- Episode: E069
- Song function: Ending reflection
- Emotional thesis: 사건이 지나간 뒤에도, 말이 멈춘 자리는 오래 젖어 있었다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "끝난 건 아닌데, 다시 시작할 말이 보이지 않았다."
- Seasonal air: empty cafe seat, water-marked bench, dawn-blurred window
- Energy level: low, numb, suspended

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: rhythm residue, dim pad, pitch-blurred motif, near-silent vocal tail
- Tempo feel: slow aftershock drift
- Vocal distance: distant-close, like speaking in an empty room
- Arrangement: leave only the afterimage of rhythm

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: empty seat, water mark, no reply, dawn window
- Point of view: inside the relationship after words stop
- Words to avoid: after breakup, new love, mourning anthem, final loss
- Do not overstate: They are not officially over.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No grief anthem, no full breakup language, no overused rainy ballad shape.

## Delivery

The song should suspend the listener in the quiet after rupture, before any decision is named.

```text
Quiet Korean aftershock reflection track, rhythm residue, dim pad, pitch-blurred motif, near-silent vocal tail, empty-room intimacy. Mood: empty cafe seat, water-marked campus bench, unanswered phone, dawn-blurred window, words stopped but the relationship not officially over. Avoid grief anthem, full breakup language, new-love hints, rainy ballad cliche, and famous song imitation.
```
