## Story Module

- Beat purpose: 접촉의 법칙을 초반 장기 복선으로 강하게 심고, 후반 청취 소실과 침묵의 완성으로 갈 감각 대비를 준비한다.
- Scene rhythm: shared task -> accidental touch -> sensory pressure -> retreat -> Arin's ordinary concern -> Seojun's private fear.
- Closing turn: 손은 금방 떨어졌지만, 서준에게는 그 짧은 접촉이 하루의 모든 소리를 다시 배열한 것처럼 남는다.

## Music Engine Module

- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional sentence: 손끝 하나가 하루의 공기를 바꾼다.
- Sonic palette: sparse piano, close breath texture, restrained strings entering only after the touch.
- Vocal direction: hushed, trembling but not melodramatic.
- Guardrails: Avoid magical-girl shimmer or fantasy spell sound. The moment should feel intimate and physical.

## Image Engine Module

- Visual mode: cinematic live-action style
- Key frames: hands reaching for the same object, sleeve brushing skin, Seojun's frozen face, Arin's ordinary puzzled look.
- Palette: bright field light suddenly muted, skin-tone warmth, pale green background blur.
- Continuity rule: The touch must look accidental and brief.

## Video Engine Module

- Recommended cut count: 10
- Sequence: object close-up -> two hands enter frame -> contact -> soundless crowd blur -> Seojun's eyes -> Arin's question -> hand withdrawal -> wide distance -> sleeve detail -> evening afterimage.
