# E033 Song Brief - 기대와 현실

## Identity

- Episode: E033
- Song function: Seojun inner monologue
- Emotional thesis: 내가 그린 장면 밖에서 네가 더 진짜였다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "상상한 빛이 꺼진 뒤에야, 네 얼굴이 제대로 보였다."
- Seasonal air: cafe window, imagined warmth versus ordinary daylight
- Energy level: low-to-mid, bittersweet and self-aware

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: split texture with soft electric piano for expectation, dry guitar and light room tone for reality
- Tempo feel: gently uneven
- Vocal distance: close, slightly wry, then softened
- Arrangement: alternate warmer and drier sections without making it gimmicky

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: window reflection, imagined frame, ordinary cup, chair gap
- Point of view: Seojun recognizing projection
- Words to avoid: reference to 500 Days, fantasy girl, disappointment, wrong answer
- Do not overstate: Arin is not failing his expectation; expectation is the problem.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No direct film or song references. Translate expectation/reality into texture contrast.

## Delivery

The song should gently puncture fantasy without making love feel foolish.

```text
Bittersweet Korean indie romance track, soft electric piano for warm imagined moments, dry guitar and light room tone for ordinary reality, close slightly self-aware vocal, gently uneven tempo. Mood: realizing the person is more real outside the imagined scene, cafe window reflection, ordinary cup, chair gap. Avoid direct film references, fantasy-girl language, blame, and melodramatic disappointment.
```
