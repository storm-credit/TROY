# E061 Song Brief - 후퇴

## Identity

- Episode: E061
- Song function: Ending reflection
- Emotional thesis: 물러난 건 마음이 아니라, 먼저 말을 꺼낼 용기였다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "멀어진 건 우리가 아니라, 먼저 부를 수 있던 목소리였다."
- Seasonal air: damp corridor, wet window, late-summer retreat
- Energy level: low, withheld, narrow

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: dry room tail, sparse piano residue, thin low pulse, withheld vocal line
- Tempo feel: slow backward step
- Vocal distance: close but hesitant
- Arrangement: leave visible gaps between phrases

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: wet window, empty corridor, closed door, delayed reply
- Point of view: relationship threshold from Seojun's restrained inner view
- Words to avoid: breakup, goodbye, forever gone, rain tears
- Do not overstate: This is avoidance, not separation.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No famous breakup ballad imitation. Avoid direct rain-piano cliche.

## Delivery

The song should feel like two people still near each other, but learning the shape of retreat.

```text
Restrained Korean drama ending-reflection track, dry room tail, sparse piano residue, thin low pulse, withheld close vocal. Mood: damp campus corridor, wet window, delayed reply, closed lecture-room door, two people retreating before they can speak. Keep phrases incomplete and quiet. Avoid breakup language, goodbye, rain-tears cliche, and famous ballad imitation.
```
