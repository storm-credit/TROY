# E096 Song Brief - 다시 읽는 아린

## Identity

- Episode: E096
- Song function: Ending reflection
- Emotional thesis: 그리움은 사람 자체보다 내가 덧씌운 의미를 먼저 걷어내야 진짜를 보게 했다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "기다리던 사람이 아니라, 끝내 다 읽지 못한 사람이 남아 있었다."
- Seasonal air: 반사면, 겹치지 않는 두 사람, 말 없는 이동, 얇은 가을빛
- Energy level: low, lucid, tender without glow

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: micro-repeat pattern, restrained strings, low electronic texture, speech-like rhythm
- Tempo feel: measured walking pace
- Vocal distance: medium-close, gentle but unsentimental
- Arrangement: no emotional bloom; let detail replace swell

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: reflection, separated silhouette, small remembered detail, pale glass
- Point of view: re-reading a person rather than idealizing a memory
- Words to avoid: she waited, she was always mine, destiny, reunion
- Do not overstate: Arin is not a healing object or a reward.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No waiting-girl fantasy, no reunion swell, no softened destiny frame.

## Delivery

The song should restore Arin as a whole person, not as a missing function in Seojun’s story.

```text
Restrained Korean autumn reflection track, micro-repeat pattern, restrained strings, low electronic texture, speech-like rhythm, medium-close gentle vocal. Mood: reflective glass, separate silhouettes, small remembered detail, pale autumn light, re-reading a person instead of idealizing a memory. Avoid waiting-girl fantasy, destiny framing, reunion warmth, healing-object lyrics, and famous song imitation.
```
