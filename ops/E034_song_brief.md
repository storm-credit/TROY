# E034 Song Brief - 아린의 닫힘

## Identity

- Episode: E034
- Song function: Seojun inner monologue
- Emotional thesis: 소리가 사라진 게 아니라, 유리 너머로 물러난 것 같았다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "너는 거기 있었는데, 소리만 유리 뒤로 멀어졌다."
- Seasonal air: window glass, muted summer edge, emotional distance
- Energy level: low, tense, blurred

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: glassy electric piano, low muted strings, airy pad, sparse brushed metal texture
- Tempo feel: slow and suspended
- Vocal distance: close but filtered, as if behind thin glass
- Arrangement: avoid full silence; use softened distance and blurred edges

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: glass, closed window, reflected face, blurred light, unanswered pause
- Point of view: Seojun facing Arin's interiority
- Words to avoid: complete silence, mute forever, disappeared, empty person
- Do not overstate: This is Arin's closing/withdrawing, not total hearing loss.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No supernatural sound effect language. Use glass and distance as metaphor.

## Delivery

The song should protect Arin's interior rather than turn her into a mystery device.

```text
Restrained Korean inner monologue ballad, glassy electric piano, low muted strings, airy pad, sparse brushed-metal texture, close filtered vocal as if behind thin glass. Mood: someone emotionally closing, sound feeling farther away but not gone, window reflection, blurred light, unanswered pause. Avoid complete silence, vanished, empty, supernatural effect language, and direct explanation.
```
