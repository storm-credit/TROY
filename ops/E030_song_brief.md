# E030 Song Brief - 대답

## Identity

- Episode: E030
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 대답은 끝이 아니라, 서로의 속도를 처음 맞추는 시작이다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "대답은 문을 닫지 않고, 천천히 열어 두었다."
- Seasonal air: cafe evening, two cups, changed street air
- Energy level: low-to-mid, warm but cautious

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: warm piano, soft guitar, small string lift, restrained ambient room tone
- Tempo feel: slow walking out of the cafe
- Vocal distance: close and warmer than E029
- Arrangement: gentle lift, unresolved ending with warmth

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: two cups, cafe door, evening street, walking gap, small nod
- Point of view: relationship transition with Arin's agency preserved
- Words to avoid: happily ever after, forever, destiny, solved, perfect answer
- Do not overstate: Relationship direction changes, but conflict is not solved.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No named-song imitation. Translate cautious yes/next step into original sound.

## Delivery

The song should feel like leaving a cafe into changed air, with the future still careful.

```text
Warm restrained Korean romance theme, cafe evening, warm piano, soft guitar, small string lift, ambient room tone, close warm vocal. Mood: receiving an answer that opens a door slowly, two cups, cafe door, evening street, walking side by side with a careful gap. Avoid happily-ever-after, destiny, forever, perfect answer, and triumphant couple-song energy.
```
