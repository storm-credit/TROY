## TROY Input Packet

- Episode: E020
- Movement: 제1악장: 얇은 봄빛

- Phase: Phase 1 - discovery and approach
- Episode logline: 체육대회 뒤풀이의 소란 끝에서 서준과 아린 사이에 아직 이름 붙일 수 없는 문이 열린다.

## Story Module

- Beat purpose: 1악장 전체를 닫고, "예외적인 현상"이 "기다리게 되는 사람"으로 바뀌었다는 감정 결론을 만든다.
- Scene rhythm: noisy table -> small exchange -> group interruption -> shared quiet -> separate walk home -> threshold image.
- Closing turn: 서준은 그날 이후 아린을 설명할 수 없게 된 것이 아니라, 설명하지 않고도 기다리게 되었다는 사실을 인정한다.

## Music Engine Module

- Song function: Ending reflection
- Emotional sentence: 하루가 끝난 뒤에도 어떤 사람의 자리만 남는다.
- Sonic palette: late-evening acoustic ballad, soft piano, faint room ambience, no dramatic climax.
- Vocal direction: restrained, reflective, slightly warmer than E005.
- Guardrails: Do not promise romance. Let the song close Phase 1 with possibility and unease.

## Image Engine Module

- Visual mode: cinematic live-action style
- Key frames: paper cups under fluorescent light, Arin across a crowded table, Seojun at the doorway, back alley light after the gathering.
- Palette: warm indoor yellow fading into cool blue night.
- Continuity rule: Cafe/night-light warmth should foreshadow later Cafe Dal lighting without naming it as destiny.

## Video Engine Module

- Recommended cut count: 10
- Sequence: table noise -> paper cup close-up -> Arin half-hidden by people -> Seojun listening -> brief shared look -> hallway exit -> night alley -> hands in pockets -> empty table -> campus gate at night.
- Motion rule: Cut from noise to stillness; let silence arrive gradually.

## Delivery Gate

- Pre-gen gate: Confirm this closes Phase 1 without starting formal dating yet.
- Post-gen gate: Remove any visual that overstates couple status.
- Release gate: Pass if the episode leaves a door open rather than delivering a conclusion.
