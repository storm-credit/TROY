# E042 Song Brief - 낯선 시선

## Identity

- Episode: E042
- Song function: Seojun inner monologue
- Emotional thesis: 지키려는 마음과 가두려는 마음은 때때로 같은 손짓을 했다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "널 지키려던 손이, 어느새 길을 막고 있었다."
- Seasonal air: campus crowd, bright heat, shadow edge
- Energy level: low-to-mid, tense but realistic

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: dry electric piano, brushed percussion, low tension pad, faint crowd room
- Tempo feel: restrained walking pulse
- Vocal distance: close, protective but uneasy
- Arrangement: small pressure build without explosion

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: crowd glance, shadow line, half-step forward, blocked path
- Point of view: Seojun's protective self-justification
- Words to avoid: villain crowd, rescue, ownership, mine
- Do not overstate: This is pressure, not crisis.

## Safe Prompt Rule

Do not imitate dramatic jealousy songs. Keep the conflict psychological and original.

## Delivery

The song should make protection feel slightly unstable.

```text
Restrained Korean inner monologue track, dry electric piano, brushed percussion, low tension pad, faint campus-crowd ambience, close uneasy vocal. Mood: wanting to protect someone in public but sensing that protection may become control, bright summer crowd, shadow line, half-step forward. Avoid rescue fantasy, ownership, mine, villain-crowd language, and dramatic conflict climax.
```
