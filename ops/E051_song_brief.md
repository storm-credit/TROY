# E051 Song Brief - 가장 행복한 날들

## Identity

- Episode: E051
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 너무 밝은 날은 그늘을 잊게 만든다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "너무 밝아서, 그늘이 있는 줄도 몰랐다."
- Seasonal air: warm summer date, bright path, ordinary happiness
- Energy level: mid, sweet but not glossy

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: warm summer groove, light bass, soft electric piano, airy percussion
- Tempo feel: relaxed date-walk tempo
- Vocal distance: close and warm
- Arrangement: keep one faint shadow under the brightness

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: summer path, same seat, shared laugh, hand nearby, small shadow
- Point of view: relationship memory with future-loss residue
- Words to avoid: forever, perfect, destiny, never-ending
- Do not overstate: This is a bright benchmark, not final safety.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No famous summer song imitation. Translate ordinary happiness into original texture.

## Delivery

The song should be bright enough that later loss has a place to return to.

```text
Warm Korean summer campus romance theme, light bass, soft electric piano, airy brushed percussion, gentle guitar color, close warm vocal. Mood: the happiest ordinary days, bright summer path, same seat, shared laugh, hand nearby, tiny shadow under the light. Avoid forever, perfect, destiny, never-ending, and glossy couple anthem energy.
```
