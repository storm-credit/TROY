# E089 Song Brief - 관계의 의미

## Identity

- Episode: E089
- Song function: Ending reflection
- Emotional thesis: 사랑이 있었다는 사실과, 돌아갈 수 없다는 사실은 함께 남았다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "사랑이 있었다는 말은, 돌아가야 한다는 뜻은 아니었다."
- Seasonal air: warm gap, different walking speeds, small exchange
- Energy level: low-to-mid, quietly accepting

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: low chamber texture, warm-but-open pad, small water-bottle tap, weak cadence
- Tempo feel: slow acceptance without closure
- Vocal distance: medium-close, calmer
- Arrangement: allow warmth, but leave the cadence weak

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: water bottle, different steps, open table, warm gap
- Point of view: separating love from return
- Words to avoid: reunion, new start, meant to be, all forgiven
- Do not overstate: Meaning is not reunion.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No romantic recovery and no final answer.

## Delivery

The song should make love and non-return coexist.

```text
Restrained Korean autumn reflection track, low chamber texture, warm-but-open pad, small water-bottle tap, weak cadence, calmer medium-close vocal. Mood: water bottle exchange, different walking speeds, open table, warm gap, love remaining without requiring return. Avoid reunion lyrics, new-start framing, meant-to-be language, all-forgiven closure, romantic recovery, and famous song imitation.
```
