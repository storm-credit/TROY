# E098 Song Brief - 그때 우리는

## Identity

- Episode: E098
- Song function: Ending reflection
- Emotional thesis: 같은 장면도 지금의 윤리로 다시 보면 전혀 다른 진실이 보였다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "그때의 우리는 거짓이 아니었지만, 그렇다고 옳았던 것도 아니었다."
- Seasonal air: 같은 공간 다른 뜻, 현재의 호흡, 마른 가을 공기
- Energy level: low-medium, clear, ethically tense

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: clear dry chords, unforced pulse, low ambient field, restrained low-end
- Tempo feel: steady reconsideration
- Vocal distance: medium-close, calmer but unsheltered
- Arrangement: keep clarity without uplift

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: room corners, renamed moment, changed angle, open ethical space
- Point of view: holding tenderness and discomfort together
- Words to avoid: perfect memory, beautiful old days, meant to happen, our season returns
- Do not overstate: Do not erase the love or romanticize the harm.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No nostalgia glow, no soft-memory montage tone, no romantic reprise.

## Delivery

The song should let truth become more complex, not more sentimental.

```text
Restrained Korean autumn reflection track, clear dry chords, unforced pulse, low ambient field, restrained low-end, medium-close calm vocal. Mood: same room with changed meaning, renamed moment, dry autumn air, tenderness and discomfort held together without sentimentality. Avoid perfect-memory framing, old-days glow, romantic reprise, and famous song imitation.
```
