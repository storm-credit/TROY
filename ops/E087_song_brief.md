# E087 Song Brief - 기억의 장소

## Identity

- Episode: E087
- Song function: Ending reflection
- Emotional thesis: 장소는 같은데, 그가 붙였던 의미만 조금씩 벗겨졌다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "장소는 아무 말도 하지 않았고, 내가 덧붙인 말만 남았다."
- Seasonal air: Hyedam bench, Cafe Dal window, old alley, bus stop
- Energy level: low, observational, spacious

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: altered motif spacing, low ambient street, dry piano felt, no nostalgic swell
- Tempo feel: slow place-walk
- Vocal distance: medium-close, less confessional
- Arrangement: keep place ambience present, avoid flashback wash

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: bench, window, alley, bus stop, passing people
- Point of view: place as memory evidence
- Words to avoid: destiny place, our beginning, time travel, perfect memory
- Do not overstate: Location holds evidence, not answers.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No pretty flashback theme and no nostalgia wallpaper.

## Delivery

The song should make the same place feel newly honest.

```text
Quiet Korean autumn place-memory track, altered motif spacing, low ambient street, dry piano felt, no nostalgic swell, medium-close reflective vocal. Mood: Hyedam bench, Cafe Dal window, old alley, bus stop, people passing through places that hold evidence but no answers. Avoid destiny-place lyrics, our-beginning framing, time-travel nostalgia, pretty flashback theme, and famous song imitation.
```
