# E026 Song Brief - 고백의 예감

## Identity

- Episode: E026
- Song function: Seojun inner monologue
- Emotional thesis: 말하기 전의 침묵이 먼저 고백이 된다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "아직 말하지 않았는데, 침묵이 먼저 달라졌다."
- Seasonal air: meeting spot, warm bench light, pre-confession pressure
- Energy level: low-to-mid, suspended anticipation

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: piano pulse, soft guitar, low ambient swell, restrained string pad
- Tempo feel: slow heartbeat without literal heartbeat sample
- Vocal distance: close, nervous, breath-aware
- Arrangement: gradual tension, no payoff explosion

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: bench, waiting path, hands, late light, rehearsed sentence
- Point of view: Seojun before speaking
- Words to avoid: proposal, forever, destiny, final answer
- Do not overstate: Keep it before the confession.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No imitation of famous confession ballads. Translate anticipation into original texture.

## Delivery

The song should live in the seconds before a person decides to say something true.

```text
Suspended Korean campus romance ballad, piano pulse, soft guitar, low ambient swell, restrained string pad, close nervous vocal. Mood: pre-confession silence changing shape, warm bench light, waiting path, rehearsed sentence, hands that do not know where to rest. Avoid proposal tone, forever, destiny, final-answer language, and dramatic payoff chorus.
```
