# E004 Song Brief

## episode

- id: E004
- title anchor: 혼란스러운 실험 / 네가 들리는 거리
- function: Seojun inner monologue
- emotional thesis: 우연인지 아닌지 끝까지 확인하고 싶다

## core feeling

- chorus emotion line: 믿고 싶지 않은 마음일수록, 더 자주 같은 곳을 돌아보게 했다
- seasonal air: cooler spring afternoon, less glow and more dry shadow
- energy level: contained, precise, increasingly obsessive

## sound direction

- sonic palette: dry piano, faint pulse, fragmented phrasing, restrained low texture
- tempo feel: measured and slightly tense, like counting steps rather than floating
- vocal distance: close male vocal, controlled and inward, almost like a private memo
- arrangement rule: keep the refrain narrow and repetitive enough to suggest checking patterns

## lyrical direction

- dominant image family: notebook edges, footsteps, crowd gaps, measured distance, turning back too often
- point of view: Seojun interior, half analytical and half unwillingly emotional
- words to avoid: destiny, miracle, obvious love, forever
- plot points not to overstate: do not explain the power system; do not make the song sound triumphant or romanticized

## short prose summary

This is the song of someone pretending he is only testing a theory, while each test makes him return to the same person more often. The track should feel like writing notes in a margin and realizing the handwriting gets messier every time her name gets close.
