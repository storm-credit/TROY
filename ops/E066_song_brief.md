# E066 Song Brief - 큰 충돌의 시작

## Identity

- Episode: E066
- Song function: Seojun inner monologue
- Emotional thesis: 충돌은 큰 소리보다, 더는 미룰 수 없는 정적에서 시작됐다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "아무도 소리치지 않았는데, 공기는 이미 부딪히고 있었다."
- Seasonal air: wet umbrella, pushed cup, corridor pressure, stopped room
- Energy level: mid, tense, withheld

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: low pressure, delayed kick, muted room tone, cut-off chord tail
- Tempo feel: slow build without drop
- Vocal distance: close but braced
- Arrangement: raise low-end pressure, but deny catharsis

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: wet umbrella, cup shift, corridor end, stopped hand
- Point of view: moment before words become damage
- Words to avoid: explosion, scream, fight to the end, goodbye
- Do not overstate: This starts the collision; it does not finish it.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No trailer rise, EDM drop, or explosive climax.

## Delivery

The song should hold the door open to rupture without walking through it yet.

```text
Intimate Korean tension track, low pressure, delayed kick, muted room tone, cut-off chord tail, close braced vocal. Mood: wet umbrella, cup pushed slightly, corridor end, stopped hand, collision beginning in silence before anyone shouts. Avoid explosive climax, EDM drop, trailer rise, scream lyrics, goodbye language, and famous song imitation.
```
