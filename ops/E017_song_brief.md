# E017 Song Brief - 족구경기

## Identity

- Episode: E017
- Song function: Seojun inner monologue
- Emotional thesis: 장난처럼 지나갈 순간이 몸에 먼저 남는다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "웃어넘긴 줄 알았는데, 몸이 먼저 기억했다."
- Seasonal air: dusty field edge, afternoon noise, embarrassed warmth
- Energy level: mid-light, awkward, self-conscious

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: muted indie pop, brushed percussion, plucked guitar, small piano motif
- Tempo feel: slightly bouncy but held back
- Vocal distance: intimate, lightly embarrassed
- Arrangement: avoid punchy hooks; let the awkward pause be part of the rhythm

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: shoelace, sideline, ball shadow, late laugh, dust
- Point of view: Seojun trying to explain away his reaction
- Words to avoid: jealousy, love, winning, heartbeat as supernatural proof
- Do not overstate: This is body awareness, not full realization.

## Safe Prompt Rule

Keep it original and small. Do not imitate a known youth-pop track.

## Delivery

The song should feel like embarrassment turning into evidence.

```text
Muted Korean indie pop inner monologue, dusty campus field edge, brushed percussion, plucked guitar, small piano motif, intimate lightly embarrassed vocal. Mood: a playful moment that the body remembers too clearly, awkward warmth, self-conscious denial. Lyrics should use shoelace, sideline, ball shadow, late laugh, and dust. Avoid jealousy, victory, big love language, and supernatural explanation.
```
