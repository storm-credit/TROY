# E025 Song Brief - 말해야 하는 이유

## Identity

- Episode: E025
- Song function: Seojun inner monologue
- Emotional thesis: 들리는 마음 앞에서도 말해야만 닿는 것이 있다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "알 것 같아도, 말하지 않으면 닿지 않았다."
- Seasonal air: corridor end, paper edge, held breath before speech
- Energy level: low, morally tense, inward

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: sparse piano, paper-rustle texture, restrained cello, low ambience
- Tempo feel: slow, hesitant
- Vocal distance: close, careful, almost confessional but not yet
- Arrangement: leave space after key lines

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: closed mouth, paper edge, corridor, folded sentence
- Point of view: Seojun confronting the limit of hearing
- Words to avoid: lecture, ethics as direct term, power, secret ability
- Do not overstate: Do not explain the system; show the need to speak.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No named-song imitation. Keep the song image-led and ethically quiet.

## Delivery

The song should make "speaking" feel necessary without sounding didactic.

```text
Quiet Korean inner monologue ballad, sparse piano, subtle paper-rustle texture, restrained cello, low ambience, close careful vocal. Mood: realizing that hearing or guessing is not the same as reaching someone, corridor end, folded sentence, held breath before speech. Lyrics should use closed mouth, paper edge, hallway distance, and words that need to be spoken. Avoid lectures, secret-ability explanation, destiny, and big confession chorus.
```
