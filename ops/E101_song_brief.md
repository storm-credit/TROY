# E101 Song Brief - 재회의 예감

## Identity

- Episode: E101
- Song function: Ending reflection
- Emotional thesis: 재회는 얼굴보다 먼저 공기의 결로 오고, 몸은 그 전조를 먼저 알아챈다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "얼굴보다 먼저 바뀐 공기 속에서, 나는 도망치지 않는 쪽을 먼저 배웠다."
- Seasonal air: 문학관 계단, 낙엽 눌린 햇빛, 멀리 보이는 익숙한 실루엣
- Energy level: low, suspended, anticipatory

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: thin piano pulses, campus air hiss, held sub-drone, delayed inhale rhythm
- Tempo feel: slowed step before contact
- Vocal distance: medium, inward but alert
- Arrangement: keep anticipation in body tension, not melodic swell

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: stairs, pocketed hand, silhouette through students, changed air
- Point of view: Seojun feeling reunion before it happens
- Words to avoid: destiny, finally, hearing again, running back
- Do not overstate: This is anticipation, not encounter.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No fate cue, no rush build, no cinematic release.

## Delivery

The song should feel like stopping one beat early because the air already warned you.

```text
Restrained Korean late-autumn reflection track, thin piano pulses, campus air hiss, held sub-drone, delayed inhale rhythm, medium inward vocal. Mood: humanities-campus stairs, fallen leaves under pale light, distant familiar silhouette, changed air before reunion, body stopping before contact. Avoid destiny language, hearing-return cues, rushing-back imagery, big swells, and famous song imitation.
```
