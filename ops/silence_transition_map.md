# Silence Transition Map

## 1. Purpose

`청취 소실`이라는 사건이 `침묵의 완성`이라는 의미로 바뀌는 과정을 단계적으로 관리한다.

## 2. Distinction

- sensory event: hearing fades
- narrative meaning: trust grows without proof

## 3. Stages

### Stage 1. Noise dependence

- Seojun still trusts hearing more than speech
- silence feels like danger

### Stage 2. Disturbed hearing

- hearing becomes unstable
- silence feels like threat and withdrawal

### Stage 3. Forced speech

- because hearing is unreliable, asking becomes unavoidable
- language starts replacing proof

### Stage 4. Bare silence

- hearing is mostly gone
- silence can still be read as loss, distance, or respect

### Stage 5. Mature silence

- silence no longer means lack
- what remains is trust without verification

## 4. Tracking Questions

- in this episode, what is heard less
- in this episode, what must be asked more
- is this silence avoidance, respect, grief, or trust
- what action clarifies the silence

## 5. Ending Lock

최종 상태:

- Seojun cannot hear the old way anymore
- the loss is real
- the maturity is also real
- silence is not emptiness
