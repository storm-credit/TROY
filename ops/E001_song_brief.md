# E001 Song Brief

## episode

- id: E001
- title anchor: 무료한 일상
- function: Seojun inner monologue
- emotional thesis: 세상은 시끄럽고, 나는 닫혀 있다

## core feeling

- chorus emotion line: 너무 많은 마음들 사이에서, 나는 내 귀부터 닫고 살았다
- seasonal air: late-spring campus light that still feels gray and distant to Seojun
- energy level: closed, overloaded, restrained, emotionally tired

## sound direction

- sonic palette: muted piano, soft noise texture, faint low ambience, very restrained arrangement
- tempo feel: slow to mid-slow, walking alone rather than flowing
- vocal distance: intimate male vocal, emotionally close but socially withdrawn
- arrangement rule: keep the track small and compressed; no hopeful lift yet

## lyrical direction

- dominant image family: earphones, gray campus, back row window, cafeteria corner, sleepless ceiling
- point of view: Seojun inward voice
- words to avoid: love confession, destiny, sudden hope, healing language
- plot points not to overstate: do not explain the full ability system; show overload through body and routine

## short prose summary

This is the song of a person who survives by closing his ears before the world can reach him. It should feel like spring light exists outside, but only a gray version of it enters his room.
