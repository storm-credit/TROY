# E060 Song Brief - 돌아볼 수 없는 선

## Identity

- Episode: E060
- Song function: Ending reflection
- Emotional thesis: 선을 넘은 건 한마디가 아니라, 말하지 않은 시간들이었다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "우리를 지나간 건 말 한마디가 아니라, 오래 닫힌 시간이었다."
- Seasonal air: late-summer heaviness, threshold, damp corridor
- Energy level: low-to-mid, ominous but restrained

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: low summer drone, sparse piano, distant percussion, damp room tone
- Tempo feel: slow threshold pulse
- Vocal distance: close but hollowed out
- Arrangement: unresolved ending, no breakup climax yet

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: line of light, closed door, heavy air, no-touch hands, wet-looking pavement
- Point of view: relationship threshold
- Words to avoid: final breakup, goodbye forever, ended, no return as melodrama
- Do not overstate: This is the threshold into Phase IV, not E078 결별.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No famous breakup-song imitation. Keep dread restrained and story-specific.

## Delivery

The song should feel like standing at a line before anyone admits it has been crossed.

```text
Restrained Korean late-summer ending-reflection track, low summer drone, sparse piano, distant percussion, damp room tone, close hollowed vocal. Mood: a relationship reaching a threshold because of long unspoken time, line of light, closed door, heavy air, no-touch hands, wet-looking pavement. Avoid final breakup language, goodbye forever, ended, melodramatic no-return statements, and famous breakup-song imitation.
```
