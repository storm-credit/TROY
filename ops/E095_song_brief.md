# E095 Song Brief - 상처 봉합

## Identity

- Episode: E095
- Song function: Ending reflection
- Emotional thesis: 같은 사건을 겪었지만, 각자의 밤은 다른 방향으로 무너졌다는 사실을 이제야 인정했다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "봉합된 건 과거가 아니라, 그 상처를 오늘에 덧씌우던 방식이었다."
- Seasonal air: 빈 복도, 바뀐 손동작, 같은 장소의 다른 얼굴
- Energy level: low, steadier, unspectacular

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: asymmetrical layers, isolated piano tone, dry sub-bass, slow breathing texture
- Tempo feel: modest forward movement, no catharsis
- Vocal distance: medium-close, steadier but not healed
- Arrangement: keep the ending small and inward

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: corridor, hand habit, closed evening room, quieter breath
- Point of view: separating old wound from current love
- Words to avoid: finally healed, fully forgiven, chapter closed, now I’m free
- Do not overstate: Yu-bin is not a cure event.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No crying catharsis ballad, no closure anthem, no merged emotional theme.

## Delivery

The song should feel like a bandage applied correctly, not like a miracle.

```text
Quiet Korean autumn reflection track, asymmetrical layers, isolated piano tone, dry sub-bass, slow breathing texture, medium-close steadier vocal. Mood: empty hallway, changed hand habit, same place different face, small inward clearing without triumph. Avoid healed slogans, chapter-closed framing, catharsis-ballad lift, cure-event tone, and famous song imitation.
```
