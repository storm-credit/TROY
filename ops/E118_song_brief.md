# E118 Song Brief - 마지막 재회

## Identity

- Episode: E118
- Song function: Ending reflection
- Emotional thesis: 마지막 재회는 진짜였음을 인정하되 함께는 가지 않는다

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "우리는 끝난 이야기를 말하고 있었는데, 이상하게도 그제야 처음으로 같은 말을 하고 있었다"
- Seasonal air: 카페 달 입구, 창가 자리, 해질녘 골목
- Energy level: low, clear, dignified

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: sparse piano, restrained strings, soft air, controlled lift
- Tempo feel: reflective final conversation
- Vocal distance: medium-close, thoughtful
- Arrangement: allow a gentle lift without ending-glow excess

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: entrance, window table, alley shadow
- Point of view: mutual truth without reunion
- Words to avoid: closure, reunion, victory, finale glow
- Do not overstate: This is approval and separation together.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No reunion declaration, no finale gloss, no melodrama.

## Delivery

The song should feel like the final shared sentence before parting.

```text
Korean dignified ending reflection, sparse piano, restrained strings, soft air, controlled lift, medium-close thoughtful vocal. Mood: Cafe Dal entrance, a window table, and an alley of long shadows where two people finally say the same truth without choosing the same future. Avoid reunion language, finale glow, closure framing, melodrama, and famous song imitation.
```
