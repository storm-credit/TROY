# E116 Song Brief - 마지막 화음의 시작

## Identity

- Episode: E116
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 마지막 재회를 앞둔 준비가 조용하게 시작된다

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "기대 대신 마지막으로 제대로 말하기를 준비했다"
- Seasonal air: 카페 달 앞 골목, 주머니 속 종이, 해질녘 그림자
- Energy level: low, poised, calm

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: sparse piano, muted low strings, dry evening air, restrained tail
- Tempo feel: careful preparation pulse
- Vocal distance: medium-close, reflective
- Arrangement: stay humble and clear

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: 골목, 종이, 그림자, 속도 조절
- Point of view: Seojun preparing to speak without hurting
- Words to avoid: reunion, climax, destiny, finale
- Do not overstate: This is preparation, not an ending answer.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No reunion glow, no finale swell, no closure cue.

## Delivery

The song should feel like the first measured breath before a final conversation.

```text
Korean late-autumn preparation track, sparse piano, muted low strings, dry evening air, restrained tail, medium-close reflective vocal. Mood: a narrow alley beside Cafe Dal, a paper note in the pocket, and the decision to slow down so the final conversation does not hurt. Avoid reunion framing, climax language, destiny turn, finale glow, and famous song imitation.
```
