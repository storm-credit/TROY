# E076 Song Brief - 마지막 대화의 준비

## Identity

- Episode: E076
- Song function: Ending reflection
- Emotional thesis: 마지막 말은 입보다 먼저 방 안의 물건들에 도착했다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "아직 말하지 않았는데, 방은 이미 끝의 모양을 알고 있었다."
- Seasonal air: cleared desk, half-open laptop, Cafe Dal exterior
- Energy level: low, still, pre-breakup dread

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: withheld refrain, soft room tone, uneven breath, unresolved table pulse
- Tempo feel: held-before-conversation
- Vocal distance: close but restrained
- Arrangement: keep refrain withheld, no swell

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: ordered desk, unclosed laptop, collar hand, empty chair
- Point of view: the breath before the last conversation
- Words to avoid: final goodbye, last forever, destiny ending, dramatic farewell
- Do not overstate: The conversation has not happened yet.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No pre-breakup trailer swell and no final-cadence language.

## Delivery

The song should make ordinary objects feel as if they know what the characters cannot yet say.

```text
Restrained Korean pre-breakup reflection track, withheld refrain, soft room tone, uneven breath, unresolved table pulse, close restrained vocal. Mood: cleared desk, half-open laptop, collar-adjusting hand, Cafe Dal exterior, empty chair before the last conversation. Avoid final-goodbye lyrics, dramatic farewell, trailer swell, destiny-ending language, and famous song imitation.
```
