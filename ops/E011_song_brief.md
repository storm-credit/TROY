# E011 Song Brief - 첫 공연

## Identity

- Episode: E011
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 사람 많은 소리 속에서 한 사람만 전경으로 떠오른다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "많은 소리 사이에서 너만 늦게까지 남았다."
- Seasonal air: thin spring evening, first stage light, campus afterglow
- Energy level: low-to-mid lift, restrained wonder

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: soft piano, clean electric guitar swells, light room ambience, restrained strings after chorus
- Tempo feel: slow walking tempo, not a performance anthem
- Vocal distance: close vocal, slightly breathy, intimate but not whisper-only
- Arrangement: small lift when the crowd becomes background and Arin becomes foreground

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: stage light, microphone dust, crowded room, one clear line of air
- Point of view: Seojun inner observation with relationship-theme framing
- Words to avoid: destiny, miracle, perfect, always, superstar
- Do not overstate: Do not say Seojun loves Arin yet; this is recognition, not confession.

## Safe Prompt Rule

Do not reference real artists, real songs, covers, translations, or imitation targets. Translate only spring-stage intimacy and the feeling of one person becoming clear in a noisy room.

## Delivery

This song should feel like the moment a public performance becomes privately unforgettable.

```text
Korean cinematic campus romance theme, late spring evening, intimate male vocal, soft piano, clean electric guitar swells, restrained strings, light room ambience. Mood: quiet wonder, one person becoming clear inside a crowded room, tender but not confessed, no dramatic climax. Lyrics should use images of stage light, dust, air, and a voice that remains after noise fades. Avoid destiny, forever, miracle, and direct plot recap.
```
