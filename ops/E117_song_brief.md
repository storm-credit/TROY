# E117 Song Brief - 끝내 피하지 않는 이유

## Identity

- Episode: E117
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 끝을 말하는 예의가 사랑의 다른 이름이 될 수 있다

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "오늘의 목적은 재시작이 아니라 종지였다"
- Seasonal air: 한강 쪽 길, 카페 달 방향, 바람이 정리하는 옷깃
- Energy level: low, steady, clear

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: sparse piano, muted air, restrained low sustain, calm tail
- Tempo feel: measured end-facing pulse
- Vocal distance: medium-close, thoughtful
- Arrangement: keep the line clear and ethical

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: 바람, 길, 방향 전환
- Point of view: Seojun choosing not to flee the ending
- Words to avoid: restart, climax, destiny, nostalgia glow
- Do not overstate: This is a statement of ending, not return.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No nostalgia bloom, no restart cue, no destiny turn.

## Delivery

The song should feel like a clean stop before the last conversation.

```text
Korean late-autumn relationship track, sparse piano, muted air, restrained low sustain, calm tail, medium-close thoughtful vocal. Mood: a road toward Cafe Dal, wind adjusting the collar, and the choice to face the ending with etiquette instead of longing. Avoid restart language, destiny turns, nostalgia glow, climax release, and famous song imitation.
```
