# E016 Song Brief - 체육대회

## Identity

- Episode: E016
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 사람 많은 곳에서 한 사람을 보지 않으려 할수록 더 많이 보게 된다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "보지 않으려던 방향에, 자꾸 하루가 기울었다."
- Seasonal air: sports field, late-spring sunlight, public noise
- Energy level: mid-light but restrained

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: light acoustic guitar, soft piano taps, restrained percussion, airy campus ambience
- Tempo feel: walking among crowds, not athletic
- Vocal distance: close vocal with a small chorus lift
- Arrangement: keep crowd energy in texture, not tempo

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: field line, water bottle, team color, bright noise, one direction of light
- Point of view: Seojun's self-denial
- Words to avoid: victory, game, champion, destiny, cheer anthem
- Do not overstate: The song is not about winning; it is about attention.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No sports anthem, no named-song imitation. Translate crowd noise into private awareness.

## Delivery

The song should make a public event feel like a private confession that has not happened yet.

```text
Restrained Korean campus romance track set during a late-spring sports day, light acoustic guitar, soft piano taps, restrained percussion, airy outdoor ambience, close vocal with small lift. Mood: trying not to look at someone in a crowd and looking anyway. Lyrics should use field line, water bottle, team color, bright noise, and one direction of light. Avoid sports anthem energy, victory language, destiny, and explicit confession.
```
