# E070 Song Brief - 돌이킬 수 없는 금

## Identity

- Episode: E070
- Song function: Ending reflection
- Emotional thesis: 같은 멜로디가 돌아왔지만, 더 이상 같은 의미로 들리지 않았다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "같은 선율이 돌아와도, 우리는 같은 자리로 돌아가지 못했다."
- Seasonal air: rainwater between tiles, blurred notebook line, blue-gray corridor
- Energy level: low, cold, unresolved

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: pitch-blurred refrain shard, damp low room, missing high note, unresolved final chord
- Tempo feel: slow unresolved threshold
- Vocal distance: close but emptied out
- Arrangement: reuse only a broken shard of the old motif

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: tile water, notebook line, doorframe split, cold blue-gray evening
- Point of view: emotional crack before official breakup
- Words to avoid: goodbye, last time, we ended, farewell cadence
- Do not overstate: This is the crack that leads toward E078, not E078 itself.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No final farewell cadence and no famous breakup-song imitation.

## Delivery

The song should leave the listener at a wet threshold with an old motif that no longer means safety.

```text
Restrained Korean threshold reflection track, pitch-blurred refrain shard, damp low room, missing high note, unresolved final chord, close emptied vocal. Mood: rainwater between floor tiles, blurred notebook line, doorframe splitting two figures, cold blue-gray corridor, an old motif returning with a changed meaning. Avoid goodbye, last-time lyrics, we-ended language, final farewell cadence, and famous song imitation.
```
