# E084 Song Brief - 혼자서 배우는 언어

## Identity

- Episode: E084
- Song function: Seojun inner monologue
- Emotional thesis: 모르는 채로 묻는 법을, 그는 처음부터 다시 배웠다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "정답을 맞히는 대신, 대답이 들어올 자리를 비워두었다."
- Seasonal air: library desk, underlined note, blank margin
- Energy level: low, careful, dry

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: short dry motif, wood-like percussion, restrained strings, careful breath gap
- Tempo feel: cautious phrase building
- Vocal distance: close, learning
- Arrangement: leave space after each phrase like a question waiting

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: note underline, blank margin, stair landing, waiting posture
- Point of view: learning language without proof
- Words to avoid: new ability, answer found, fixed, enlightened
- Do not overstate: Asking is practice, not solution.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No bright growth montage and no magical learning cue.

## Delivery

The song should feel like a person learning to leave room for another voice.

```text
Restrained Korean self-relearning track, short dry motif, wood-like percussion, restrained strings, careful breath gaps, close learning vocal. Mood: library desk, underlined note, blank margin, stair landing, a question leaving room for another voice. Avoid new-ability lyrics, answer-found framing, fixed/enlightened language, bright growth montage, and famous song imitation.
```
