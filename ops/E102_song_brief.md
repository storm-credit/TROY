# E102 Song Brief - 다시 만난 두 사람

## Identity

- Episode: E102
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 평범한 인사와 종이 한 장의 다리가, 확실함보다 먼저 관계를 다시 열었다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "닿은 손끝보다, 그 뒤에도 무너지지 않은 공기가 더 이상했다."
- Seasonal air: 도서관 앞, 과제 종이, 짧은 손끝 접촉, 맑은 늦가을 오후
- Energy level: low, careful, slightly eased

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: dry piano, paper-rustle texture, soft pad, light restrained pulse
- Tempo feel: cautious step matching
- Vocal distance: close and level, no dramatic ache
- Arrangement: let ordinary civility carry the intimacy

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: paper bridge, held gaze, spoken name, side-by-side pause
- Point of view: two people testing whether ordinariness can still hold them
- Words to avoid: miracle, meant to be, embrace, signal returned
- Do not overstate: Contact does not equal clarity.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No duet swell, no cathartic hug tone, no instant-warmth chorus.

## Delivery

The song should feel like a careful greeting that matters because nothing explodes.

```text
Korean relationship theme with dry piano, paper-rustle texture, soft pad, light restrained pulse, close level vocal. Mood: library front, cautious greeting, one sheet of paper bridging distance, fingertip contact that changes nothing and everything a little. Avoid miracle framing, destiny warmth, embrace imagery, hearing-return cues, dramatic duet buildup, and famous song imitation.
```
