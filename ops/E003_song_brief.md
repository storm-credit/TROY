# E003 Song Brief

## episode

- id: E003
- title anchor: 아린이라는 존재
- function: Seojun inner monologue
- emotional thesis: 관심은 이미 시작됐는데 인정만 늦는다

## core feeling

- chorus emotion line: 모른 척하려 할수록, 마음은 먼저 그쪽을 보고 있었다
- seasonal air: late-spring afternoon with a light breeze, no humidity yet
- energy level: restrained, inward, quietly shifting

## sound direction

- sonic palette: intimate piano, soft ambient air, very light low strings, spare arrangement
- tempo feel: slow to medium-slow, hesitant pulse rather than a steady groove
- vocal distance: close male vocal, as if speaking to himself rather than performing outward
- arrangement rule: no explosive chorus, let the melody open only slightly in the refrain

## lyrical direction

- dominant image family: bench, wind lifting fabric, yellow cardigan, thin light, a body turning before the mind agrees
- point of view: Seojun first-person or near-first-person emotional interior
- words to avoid: destiny, forever, love confession, miracle
- plot points not to overstate: do not explain the hearing power directly; do not claim certainty about Arin

## short prose summary

This is the song of someone who keeps pretending nothing has changed, while every small movement in his body has already started leaning toward one person. The song should feel like noticing spring air only after it has already brushed past your sleeve.
