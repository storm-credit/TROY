# E024 Song Brief - 기다리는 사람

## Identity

- Episode: E024
- Song function: Ending reflection
- Emotional thesis: 네가 없는 시간이 너를 가장 선명하게 만든다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "빈자리가 먼저 와서, 네 이름 없는 시간을 채웠다."
- Seasonal air: late-afternoon campus room, empty chair, still window
- Energy level: low, reflective, tender

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: soft piano, minimal guitar, warm pad, faint room tone
- Tempo feel: slow waiting pulse
- Vocal distance: close and gentle
- Arrangement: sparse verses, soft unresolved ending

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: empty chair, clock, window light, unsent message, hallway
- Point of view: Seojun recognizing waiting through absence
- Words to avoid: loneliness melodrama, forever, destiny, can't live without you
- Do not overstate: This is early longing, not devastation.

## Safe Prompt Rule

Original mood only. Avoid copying late-night ballad conventions too closely.

## Delivery

The song should feel like waiting becoming visible in an empty chair.

```text
Soft Korean reflective ballad, late-afternoon campus room, soft piano, minimal guitar, warm pad, faint room tone, close gentle vocal. Mood: realizing someone is missed through an empty chair and still window light, early longing, tender restraint. Lyrics should use empty chair, clock, window light, unsent message, and hallway. Avoid melodrama, destiny, forever, and can't-live-without-you language.
```
