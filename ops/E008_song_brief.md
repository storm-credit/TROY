# E008 Song Brief

## episode

- id: E008
- title anchor: 도서관 첫 미팅
- function: relationship theme
- emotional thesis: 둘만 남은 시간은 생각보다 조용하다

## core feeling

- chorus emotion line: 낮은 목소리로도 충분한 날이 있었다, 가까워짐은 늘 그렇게 조용했다
- seasonal air: clear late-spring afternoon filtered through library glass
- energy level: low, tender, quietly suspended

## sound direction

- sonic palette: intimate piano, brushed drums, soft room air, minimal low-end support
- tempo feel: slow to mid-slow, as if the song is careful not to disturb the room
- vocal distance: close and hushed, almost shared-breath distance
- arrangement rule: avoid any explosive lift; the chorus should feel fuller only by intimacy, not volume

## lyrical direction

- dominant image family: book shadows, window light, chair legs, low voices, held silence
- point of view: relational voice with equal attention to shared quiet and unequal understanding
- words to avoid: forever, miracle, cinematic destiny phrasing, overblown longing
- plot points not to overstate: do not flatten the library scene into a plot beat; make the quiet itself the event

## short prose summary

This is the song of discovering that being left alone together does not create panic, but a kind of low-lit calm that both people remember differently. The song should feel like light resting on two pages at once.

## Suno-ready prompt

```text
Korean intimate relationship theme, close hushed vocal, piano with brushed drums, late-spring library window light, low voices and shared silence, book shadows, delicate emotional closeness, calm and cinematic, no grand confession, no dramatic swell
```

## guardrails

- let the quiet carry the emotion
- do not make the track sound empty; stillness should feel alive
- keep the emotional job singular: quiet co-presence becoming memorable
