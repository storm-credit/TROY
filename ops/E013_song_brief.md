## Identity

- Episode: E013
- Song function: Seojun inner monologue
- Emotional thesis: 같은 얼굴에서 전과 다른 결을 느끼고도, 그는 아직 그것을 사람의 변화로 받아들이지 못한다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "같은 빛인데, 어제와는 다른 곳이 떨렸다."
- Seasonal air: thin morning light, campus bench, cautious curiosity
- Energy level: low-to-mid, analytical but softened

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: delicate piano motif, plucked acoustic guitar, subtle string texture
- Tempo feel: measured, observant
- Vocal distance: close but slightly withheld
- Arrangement: small repeated motif with slight variation each chorus

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: same window, changed light, paper edge, small tremor
- Point of view: Seojun noticing too much and understanding too little
- Words to avoid: system, anomaly, exception, data, experiment
- Do not overstate: Do not explain the rhythm rule directly in lyrics.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No direct imitation or translation of existing songs. Translate "variation" into image and texture.
