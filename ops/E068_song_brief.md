# E068 Song Brief - rupture

## Identity

- Episode: E068
- Song function: Relationship rupture theme
- Emotional thesis: 같은 노래라고 믿었던 시간이, 서로 다른 박자로 갈라졌다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "나만 같은 노래를 듣고 있었다는 말이, 끝내 유리처럼 갈라졌다."
- Seasonal air: rain-blurred neon, cracked reflection, missing frequency
- Energy level: mid, structurally broken, no catharsis

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: sudden band loss, cut reverb tail, broken loop, low empty room
- Tempo feel: loop that stops breathing
- Vocal distance: close, then abruptly hollowed
- Arrangement: create rupture by subtraction, not explosion

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: cracked reflection, blurred neon, loose earphone line, different directions
- Point of view: relationship asymmetry finally named
- Words to avoid: final breakup, goodbye, torn photo, never again
- Do not overstate: Rupture makes breakup inevitable later, but it is not E078.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No explosive climax. No famous breakup song imitation.

## Delivery

The song should feel like a frequency has gone missing from a once-familiar melody.

```text
Korean relationship-rupture theme built by subtraction, sudden band loss, cut reverb tail, broken loop, low empty room, close vocal that becomes hollow. Mood: cracked reflection, rain-blurred neon, loose earphone line, two people in the same frame looking different directions, a familiar melody missing one frequency. Avoid final breakup language, goodbye, torn-photo imagery, explosive climax, and famous song imitation.
```
