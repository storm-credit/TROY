# E014 Song Brief - 음악 이야기

## Identity

- Episode: E014
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 음악 이야기는 취향 설명이 아니라 서로의 세계를 조심스럽게 열어 보이는 방식이 된다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "네가 좋아한 소리 옆에, 내 침묵도 잠깐 앉았다."
- Seasonal air: cafe afternoon, notebook paper, quiet sunlight
- Energy level: low, warm, conversational

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: warm upright piano, nylon guitar, soft brushed percussion, minimal bass
- Tempo feel: conversational sway
- Vocal distance: duet-like intimacy, but one vocal is enough
- Arrangement: gentle call-and-response phrasing without theatrical duet drama

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: notebook margin, half-finished coffee, sunlight on table, shared song without naming it
- Point of view: relationship space, not pure Seojun monologue
- Words to avoid: artist names, song titles, masterpiece, genre lecture, symphony jargon
- Do not overstate: Do not make it sound like they already share one complete world.

## Safe Prompt Rule

Do not ask for the style of a famous song or artist. Use atmosphere only: cafe warmth, shy musical conversation, and original melody.

## Delivery

The song should feel like two people talking about music while actually talking about loneliness.

```text
Warm Korean acoustic romance theme, cafe afternoon, upright piano, nylon guitar, soft brushed percussion, minimal bass, gentle conversational vocal. Mood: two people discussing music while revealing small parts of themselves, shy warmth, paper and sunlight. Lyrics should use notebook margins, table light, unfinished coffee, and a song left unnamed. Avoid artist names, song titles, music-theory jargon, and big romantic promises.
```
