# E106 Song Brief - 재접속

## Identity

- Episode: E106
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 다시 가까워지는 것보다, 다시 안전하게 말할 방법을 만드는 일이 먼저였다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "가까워지자는 말보다, 불편하면 말하자는 약속이 더 진짜였다."
- Seasonal air: 마주 앉은 거리, 정해지는 신호, 늦가을 실내 공기, 연습 같은 대화
- Energy level: low-medium, deliberate, stable

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: restrained piano ostinato, clean low pad, quiet percussive taps, still room air
- Tempo feel: measured rule-setting pulse
- Vocal distance: two-person near but unsentimental
- Arrangement: prioritize structure and calm over longing

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: measured table distance, stop signal, not pretending to be okay, slower method
- Point of view: a relationship being rebuilt through operating rules rather than romance
- Words to avoid: second chance destiny, sweeter than before, happily again, perfect fit
- Do not overstate: Reconnection here is method, not label.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No second-chance anthem, no romantic reboot cue, no polished happy-end tone.

## Delivery

The song should make careful rule-setting feel more intimate than a confession.

```text
Korean late-autumn relationship track, restrained piano ostinato, clean low pad, quiet percussive taps, still room air, near but unsentimental vocal. Mood: two people designing safer ways to talk, naming discomfort, setting stop signals, refusing to pretend everything is okay. Avoid second-chance destiny language, sweeter-than-before framing, relationship label certainty, bright reboot lift, and famous song imitation.
```
