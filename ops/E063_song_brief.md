# E063 Song Brief - 말할 수 없는 진실

## Identity

- Episode: E063
- Song function: Seojun inner monologue
- Emotional thesis: 말하지 못한 진실은, 때로 거짓말보다 차갑게 들린다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "목까지 온 말은, 끝내 다른 말이 되어 식었다."
- Seasonal air: damp notebook edge, glass reflection, swallowed breath
- Energy level: low, intimate, unresolved

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: incomplete piano phrases, breath-close vocal, missing chord resolution, damp hammer tone
- Tempo feel: slow phrase that stops before landing
- Vocal distance: very close, almost spoken
- Arrangement: let lines enter and fall away before completion

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: swallowed words, dim phone, wet paper edge, reflected face
- Point of view: Seojun unable to explain what he heard
- Words to avoid: confession, secret power, truth revealed, magic
- Do not overstate: The ability must remain unspoken.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No direct ability exposition in lyrics. Use absence and unfinished phrases.

## Delivery

The song should feel like a true sentence being replaced by a colder safe sentence.

```text
Minimal Korean inner-monologue track, incomplete piano phrases, breath-close almost-spoken vocal, damp piano hammer tone, missing chord resolution. Mood: swallowed words, damp notebook edge, dim phone, glass reflection, a true sentence replaced by a colder safe sentence. Avoid confession release, secret-power wording, magic, truth-revealed lyrics, and famous song imitation.
```
