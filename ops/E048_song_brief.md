# E048 Song Brief - 말보다 빠른 해석

## Identity

- Episode: E048
- Song function: Seojun inner monologue
- Emotional thesis: 맞힌 줄 알았던 말이, 네 문장을 끊고 있었다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "맞는 말이라 믿은 순간, 네 말이 멈췄다."
- Seasonal air: cafe window line, interrupted sentence, dry summer room
- Energy level: low-to-mid, clear tension

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: dry piano, interrupted percussion, low bass shadow, clipped room tone
- Tempo feel: stop-start pulse
- Vocal distance: close, regret not yet fully conscious
- Arrangement: use brief cutoffs to mirror interruption

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: interrupted mouth, closed notebook, window line, hand retracting
- Point of view: Seojun realizing too late
- Words to avoid: villain, betrayal, accusation, mind-reading reveal
- Do not overstate: This is a first clear crack, not the rupture.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No dramatic breakup imitation. Keep the crack small but audible.

## Delivery

The song should sound like a sentence cut off in the middle.

```text
Restrained Korean inner monologue tension track, dry piano, interrupted percussion, low bass shadow, clipped room tone, close vocal with late regret. Mood: thinking you said the right thing and realizing you stopped someone else's sentence, closed notebook, window line, hand retracting. Avoid villain language, betrayal, accusation, mind-reading reveal, and full breakup climax.
```
