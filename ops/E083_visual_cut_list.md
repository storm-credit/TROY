3. Message delete: phone screen angled away, no readable text, only thumb hesitation.
4. Side glance: Seojun glances at the empty seat beside him.
5. Empty seat: seat remains ordinary, not haunted.
6. Hand repeat: close shot of fingers tapping the same pattern twice.
7. No answer: his face waits for a response that does not arrive.
8. Breath pause: a small inhale in a gray hallway.
9. Habit recognized: wide shot of him lowering his hand instead of guessing.
