# E035 Song Brief - 다시 맞춘 호흡

## Identity

- Episode: E035
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 다시 맞춘다는 건 처음부터 같았다는 뜻이 아니다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "같지 않은 숨을, 우리는 천천히 다시 놓아 보았다."
- Seasonal air: open window, softened hallway, evening after awkwardness
- Energy level: low-to-mid, repairing but fragile

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: warm electric piano, soft guitar, brushed percussion with more air, faint bass movement
- Tempo feel: slow breathing pulse
- Vocal distance: close duet-like warmth with one vocal
- Arrangement: small lift, no full resolution

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: open window, breath, side-by-side reset, evening corridor
- Point of view: relationship repair
- Words to avoid: fixed, solved, perfect again, never fight
- Do not overstate: The central flaw is not healed.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No imitation. Keep the repair partial and human.

## Delivery

The song should feel like opening a window after a room got too still.

```text
Gentle Korean relationship theme, warm electric piano, soft guitar, airy brushed percussion, faint bass movement, close warm vocal. Mood: two people slowly re-matching their breathing after awkward distance, open window, evening corridor, fragile repair. Avoid fixed, solved, perfect again, never fight, and big reconciliation climax.
```
