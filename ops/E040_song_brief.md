# E040 Song Brief - 첫 키스

## Identity

- Episode: E040
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 가까워진 숨이 모든 대답은 아니었다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "숨이 가까워져도, 마음을 다 안다는 뜻은 아니었다."
- Seasonal air: summer night edge, close faces, after-silence
- Energy level: mid, intimate and held

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: intimate electric piano, warm strings used sparingly, soft night ambience, slow bass movement
- Tempo feel: slow night pulse
- Vocal distance: close and breath-aware
- Arrangement: one emotional swell, then pull back into space

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: night path, near faces, hands still, light cutaway, widened frame
- Point of view: relationship threshold with future ambiguity
- Words to avoid: possession, mine, forever, destiny, complete, all answers
- Do not overstate: First kiss is tender but not final understanding.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No famous kiss-scene ballad imitation. Keep consent, restraint, and ambiguity.

## Delivery

The song should be intimate, but leave air around the moment.

```text
Intimate Korean cinematic romance theme, slow summer night pulse, intimate electric piano, sparse warm strings, soft night ambience, slow bass movement, close breath-aware vocal. Mood: first kiss as a tender threshold, near faces, still hands, light cutaway, after-silence, widened frame. Avoid possession, mine, forever, destiny, complete, all-answers language, and triumphant romantic climax.
```
