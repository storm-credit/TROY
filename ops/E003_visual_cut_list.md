# E003 Visual Cut List

## episode

- id: E003
- title anchor: 아린이라는 존재
- song function: Seojun inner monologue
- MV type: reluctant attraction, character recognition
- recommended still count: 10

## visual thesis

서준은 아직 인정하지 않지만, 그의 시선과 몸은 이미 아린이 있는 방향을 먼저 기억한다.

## master look prompt

```text
Cinematic 16:9 still for episode 3 of a Korean campus romance.
A quiet 22-year-old Korean male student with a guarded face, charcoal hoodie, wired earphones, understated campus fashion.
A bright but unforced Korean female student in a yellow cardigan with natural hair and an open expression.
Late-spring campus atmosphere, thin sunlight, moving air, soft young greenery, red brick and stone campus textures, emotional hesitation rather than direct romance.
Keep the same Seojun face design, hoodie silhouette, and wired earphones from previous episodes.
Keep Arin visually clear, warm, and real, never dreamy or fantasy-like.
No text, no logo, no watermark.
```

- style exploration: Midjourney
- production stills: Nano Banana
- hero refinement if needed: Imagen

## cut list

### cut 1. 문향관 앞 바람

- purpose: 아린의 존재를 장소와 공기 먼저 소개
- shot: wide
- motion intent: slow drift with wind

prompt:
```text
Wide cinematic shot of a Korean university literature building courtyard in late spring, red brick walls, trees casting soft shadows, a light breeze moving leaves and papers, one female student in a yellow cardigan near a bench, thin afternoon sunlight, poetic and realistic, 16:9
```

### cut 2. 멀리서 노래를 듣는 서준

- purpose: 소리보다 먼저 몸이 반응하는 순간
- shot: medium-wide from behind
- motion intent: gentle pause

prompt:
```text
Rear three-quarter shot of a quiet Korean male student in a charcoal hoodie and wired earphones stopping on a campus path as if something distant caught him, late-spring light, subtle wind, emotional hesitation, cinematic 16:9
```

### cut 3. 노란 가디건 디테일

- purpose: 아린을 기억하게 만드는 시각적 표지
- shot: insert close-up
- motion intent: fabric flutter

prompt:
```text
Close-up detail of a yellow cardigan sleeve moving lightly in spring wind beside a campus bench, warm sunlight on fabric, soft natural realism, 16:9
```

### cut 4. 벤치와 아린의 옆모습

- purpose: 아린을 처음으로 한 사람의 결로 인식
- shot: medium profile
- motion intent: stillness with light breeze

prompt:
```text
Profile shot of a young Korean woman sitting by a campus bench near a literature building garden, yellow cardigan, notebook on lap, open but calm expression, late afternoon spring light, realistic and quietly luminous, 16:9
```

### cut 5. 책장 아닌 사람 쪽으로 가는 시선

- purpose: E002의 서점 예외가 사람 인식으로 전환됨
- shot: Seojun close medium
- motion intent: slight rack focus

prompt:
```text
Close medium shot of a guarded Korean male student's face as his eyes shift off-frame toward someone, charcoal hoodie, wired earphones, sunlight touching one cheek, emotional curiosity he is trying to hide, cinematic 16:9
```

### cut 6. 멀어진 거리의 투샷

- purpose: 아직 멀지만 이미 연결된 둘의 거리감
- shot: wide two-shot
- motion intent: static with environmental motion

prompt:
```text
Wide shot of two Korean university students separated by distance in a campus courtyard, the male student standing still, the female student near a bench and trees, spring wind moving around them, no direct interaction yet, emotional pull across space, 16:9
```

### cut 7. 공책 가장자리

- purpose: 문학적이고 생활적인 세부 감각 강화
- shot: insert
- motion intent: page flutter

prompt:
```text
Close-up of a notebook edge and loose paper trembling in a light spring breeze on a campus bench, handwritten notes, warm and delicate realism, 16:9
```

### cut 8. 서준의 부정

- purpose: 마음은 기울지만 표정은 닫힌 상태 유지
- shot: front medium close-up
- motion intent: hold

prompt:
```text
Front medium close-up of a quiet Korean male student trying to keep a blank expression, charcoal hoodie, wired earphones, late spring light, inner conflict visible only in the eyes, restrained and cinematic, 16:9
```

### cut 9. 스쳐 가는 순간

- purpose: 실제 거리의 최초 축소
- shot: shoulder-level passing shot
- motion intent: slow cross

prompt:
```text
Shoulder-level cinematic shot of two Korean university students passing close by each other on a campus path, yellow cardigan and charcoal hoodie, light breeze, thin sunlight, no spoken exchange, emotional awareness in silence, 16:9
```

### cut 10. 뒤늦게 돌아보는 엔딩

- purpose: 인정은 늦지만 몸은 이미 돌아본다는 엔딩
