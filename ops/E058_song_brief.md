# E058 Song Brief - 잘못 읽은 마음

## Identity

- Episode: E058
- Song function: Seojun inner monologue
- Emotional thesis: 맞다고 믿은 소리가, 네 마음을 빗나갔다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "분명히 들은 것 같았는데, 네 마음은 그곳에 없었다."
- Seasonal air: low room light, hand withdrawal, wrong interpretation
- Energy level: low-to-mid, painful and direct

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: clipped piano, low pulse, broken percussion, dry room tone
- Tempo feel: uneven and contained
- Vocal distance: close, stunned, late-realizing
- Arrangement: broken rhythmic gaps, no thriller sound design

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: withdrawing hand, interrupted face, table gap, low light
- Point of view: Seojun facing the consequence of over-reading
- Words to avoid: mind reader, supernatural, betrayal, villain, evidence
- Do not overstate: This is damage, not final rupture.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No thriller or supernatural prompt. Keep the pain relational.

## Delivery

The song should make being wrong feel physically quiet.

```text
Painful restrained Korean inner monologue, clipped piano, low pulse, broken percussion, dry room tone, close stunned vocal. Mood: realizing a confident interpretation missed the other person's heart, withdrawing hand, interrupted face, table gap, low light. Avoid mind-reader, supernatural, betrayal, villain, evidence, and final-breakup climax.
```
