# E023 Song Brief - 작은 질투

## Identity

- Episode: E023
- Song function: Seojun inner monologue
- Emotional thesis: 확인하려던 마음이 사실은 놓치기 싫다는 마음이었다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "괜찮은 척한 자리에서, 손 안의 컵만 조금 구겨졌다."
- Seasonal air: campus group table, dry afternoon, social noise
- Energy level: low-to-mid, tense but contained

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: muted guitar, dry piano, small pulse, restrained low strings
- Tempo feel: held-back mid tempo
- Vocal distance: close, self-denying, not angry
- Arrangement: small tension in the pre-chorus, no aggressive drop

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: paper cup, group laugh, side glance, chair gap, dry light
- Point of view: Seojun disguising jealousy as analysis
- Words to avoid: jealousy as direct label, possession, rival, mine, anger
- Do not overstate: No villain framing for the other person.

## Safe Prompt Rule

Do not imitate pop jealousy songs. Translate quiet discomfort into restrained texture.

## Delivery

The song should feel like the first small crease in a paper cup.

```text
Restrained Korean indie inner monologue, muted guitar, dry piano, small pulse, restrained low strings, close self-denying vocal. Mood: small jealousy disguised as checking the situation, social noise at a campus table, dry afternoon light. Lyrics should use paper cup, group laughter, side glance, chair gap, and a hand gripping too tightly. Avoid possessive language, anger, rival framing, and direct jealousy labels.
```
