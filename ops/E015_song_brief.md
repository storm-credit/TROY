# E015 Song Brief - 연결되는 마음

## Identity

- Episode: E015
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 가까워진다는 것은 큰 사건이 아니라 하루의 리듬에 한 사람이 들어오는 일이다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "하루의 빈칸에 네가 조용히 들어왔다."
- Seasonal air: soft spring routine, hallway light, late class
- Energy level: low-to-mid, warm accumulation

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: acoustic guitar pulse, piano droplets, warm pad, very light percussion
- Tempo feel: gentle walking pulse
- Vocal distance: close, more relaxed than E013
- Arrangement: gradual layering, no explosive chorus

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: hallway, timetable, paper cup, repeating footsteps
- Point of view: Seojun noticing routine change
- Words to avoid: fate, soulmate, completed, perfect connection
- Do not overstate: Keep the connection fragile and pre-confessional.

## Safe Prompt Rule

Avoid copying romantic K-ballad tropes too closely. Build an original campus routine song.

## Delivery

The song should feel like a person becoming part of the listener's daily weather.

```text
Gentle Korean campus acoustic song, soft spring routine, acoustic guitar pulse, piano droplets, warm pad, very light percussion, close relaxed vocal. Mood: someone quietly entering the empty spaces of a day, fragile connection before confession. Lyrics should use hallway light, timetable, paper cup, footsteps, and repeated ordinary moments. Avoid fate, soulmate, perfect love, and big dramatic chorus.
```
