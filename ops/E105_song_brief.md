# E105 Song Brief - 남겨진 감정

## Identity

- Episode: E105
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 남겨진 것은 재결합의 확신이 아니라, 모르면 묻겠다는 낮은 약속이다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "다시라는 말 대신, 모르면 묻겠다는 약속이 우리 사이에 남았다."
- Seasonal air: 카페 밖 찬 바람, 삼켜지는 단어, 얇은 저녁 공기 속 고개 끄덕임
- Energy level: low, steady, newly grounded

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: cold-air piano, thin strings, grounded pulse, open tail
- Tempo feel: slow walk after conversation
- Vocal distance: medium-close, plainspoken
- Arrangement: keep sweetness low; the weight should sit in the promise

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: doorway air, stopped word, nod instead of embrace, separate return path
- Point of view: two people leaving with less fantasy and more method
- Words to avoid: restart, forever, healed, love restored
- Do not overstate: This is a working promise, not reunion confirmation.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No restart anthem, no forever hook, no warm closure swell.

## Delivery

The song should feel like a promise that sounds plain but lasts longer than a dramatic vow.

```text
Korean relationship theme in cold evening air, piano, thin strings, grounded pulse, open unresolved tail, medium-close plain vocal. Mood: cafe exit, almost saying again, changing the sentence into a promise to ask and speak before closing, low stable calm without reunion certainty. Avoid restart language, forever promises, healed-love framing, big romantic lift, and famous song imitation.
```
