# E010 Song Brief

## episode

- id: E010
- title anchor: 특별한 관심
- function: ending reflection
- emotional thesis: 예외는 확정됐고, 이제 관계가 시작된다

## core feeling

- chorus emotion line: 이름을 붙이지 못한 채로도, 마음은 이미 다음 쪽으로 기울고 있었다
- seasonal air: spring dusk with longer shadows and thinning light
- energy level: lifted but restrained, anticipatory, unresolved

## sound direction

- sonic palette: brighter piano motif, restrained warmth, soft ambient air, minimal rhythm support
- tempo feel: mid-slow with a slight forward lean
- vocal distance: close male vocal with a little more openness than prior ending reflections
- arrangement rule: let the track end with direction, not closure

## lyrical direction

- dominant image family: path, shadow, shoulder turn, dusk air, a name almost formed
- point of view: Seojun inward reflection with a small outward tilt toward the future
- words to avoid: dating announcement, forever, certainty, over-bright happy ending tone
- plot points not to overstate: this is not payoff or confession; it is threshold recognition

## short prose summary

This is the song of someone who still cannot name what is happening, but can no longer pretend nothing is happening. The song should feel like a path at dusk that has become directional.

## Suno-ready prompt

```text
Korean ending reflection track, brighter piano motif with restrained hopeful lift, intimate male vocal, spring dusk campus path, longer shadows, special attention becoming direction, tender and uncertain, cinematic, no confession, no grand resolution
```

## guardrails

- keep the forward tilt subtle
- the track should close Phase 1 without sounding like an ending
- the emotional job is orientation lock, not romantic payoff
