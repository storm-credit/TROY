# Visual Bible

## 1. Director Decision

이 작품의 기본 영상미는 `애니`가 아니라 `시네마틱 실사풍`이다.

고정 원칙:
- base mode: cinematic live-action style
- emotional texture: Korean youth-film stills and restrained music-video frames
- support mode: limited illustrated or semi-animated treatment only for memory, inner hearing, or symbolic inserts

즉, 전편을 애니풍으로 통일하지 않는다.

## 2. Why Cinematic Wins

- 이 작품은 표정의 미세한 흔들림, 공기의 밀도, 빛의 각도, 거리감이 중요하다
- 이야기의 힘이 판타지 비주얼보다 생활감 있는 잔향에서 나온다
- `500 Days of Summer`, `Architecture 101`, `Cyrano Agency` 계열의 모티브와 더 잘 맞는다
- 아름다운 이별 결말은 과장된 스타일보다 현실에 가까운 질감에서 더 오래 남는다

## 3. Base Visual Language

### face and body

- realistic Korean young-adult faces
- no exaggerated anime eye ratio
- no idol-gloss perfection
- small facial changes matter more than dramatic posing

### camera feeling

- quiet medium shots
- patient close-ups
- occasional wide shots for distance and season
- movement should feel observed, not flashy

### image texture

- soft filmic realism
- natural skin texture
- restrained color separation
- light grain allowed, but not retro fetishized

## 4. Color And Seasonal Palette

### phase 1-2

- thin spring light
- gray-blue restraint
- pale green accents
- soft yellow entering slowly through Arin

### phase 3

- brighter highlights
- warmer skin and air
- denser summer light
- saturated but never candy-like

### phase 4

- humid late-summer heaviness
- softened edges
- darker greens and damp grays
- spill, blur, and shadow interruption

### phase 5

- transparent autumn air
- cleaner contrast
- longer shadows
- warm neutrals, dry light, and calm sky tones

### phase 6

- late-autumn clarity
- lean palette
- less color noise
- quiet gold, pale brown, cold blue, and honest skin tones

## 5. Framing Rules

- use wide shots to show emotional distance
- use close shots only when the emotion has nowhere else to hide
- avoid overusing symmetrical romance framing
- empty space is part of the composition
- windows, corridors, benches, stairs, and river edges should carry emotional geometry

## 6. Animation Allowance

애니 또는 일러스트풍은 `예외적 장치`로만 허용한다.

allowed uses:
- inner hearing motifs
- memory fragments
- symbolic overlays such as waveform, paper, wind, or piano-shadow traces
- end-card or lyric-video style inserts

not allowed:
- full-episode anime styling
- chibi, fantasy gloss, comic-book exaggeration
- visual language that makes the story feel weightless

## 7. Tool Strategy

### Midjourney

- use for style exploration
- use for palette or framing exploration
- do not rely on it as the main continuity engine

### Nano Banana

- use as the main production still engine
- use image-to-image continuity for recurring characters, outfits, and places
- default engine for episode MV still generation

### Imagen

- use for hero-shot refinement
- use sparingly for poster-grade or anchor images
- not the main batch-production tool

## 8. Character Visual Rule

### Seojun

- charcoal, navy, washed neutrals
- closed shoulders, guarded posture
- realism first
- even when handsome, he should feel lived-in rather than styled

### Arin

- clarity enters through her, but she is not fantasy-lit
- softer warm notes, cleaner silhouette
- yellow or pale warm accents can function as memory markers
- open expression matters more than beauty stylization

## 9. MV House Rule

- each episode MV should feel like memory fragments, not full plot recap
- stills should hold emotional residue
- the final cut must usually leave after-feeling rather than resolution
- beauty is welcome, but atmosphere comes before prettiness

## 10. One-Line Summary

`너라는 운율`의 영상미는 `현실의 표정 위에 음악의 잔향이 얹히는 시네마틱 실사풍`이다.
