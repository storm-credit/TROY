# E044 Song Brief - 다른 방식의 다정함

## Identity

- Episode: E044
- Song function: Seojun inner monologue
- Emotional thesis: 다른 다정함을 본 순간, 내 다정함의 모양이 낯설어졌다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "네가 흔들린 게 아니라, 내가 내 모양을 잃었다."
- Seasonal air: campus table, calm gesture, dry summer shade
- Energy level: low, inward comparison

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: muted electric piano, dry guitar, soft low pulse, thin room air
- Tempo feel: slow and watchful
- Vocal distance: close, subdued, self-aware
- Arrangement: no dramatic swell; let discomfort remain quiet

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: cup handoff, neutral smile, background shadow, table gap
- Point of view: Seojun comparing forms of kindness
- Words to avoid: better man, stolen, chosen, villain, competition
- Do not overstate: Arin is not a prize and Dohyun is not coded as bad.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No love-triangle pop imitation. Translate comparison into texture.

## Delivery

The song should sound like self-doubt, not a rival scene.

```text
Quiet Korean inner monologue, muted electric piano, dry guitar, soft low pulse, thin room air, close subdued vocal. Mood: seeing another person's calm kindness and suddenly doubting the shape of your own, cup handoff, neutral smile, table gap, background shadow. Avoid better-man, stolen-love, villain, competition, and melodramatic triangle tropes.
```
