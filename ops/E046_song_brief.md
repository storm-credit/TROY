# E046 Song Brief - 행복의 리듬

## Identity

- Episode: E046
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 다시 맞은 리듬이 너무 달아서, 틀릴 수도 있다는 생각을 잊었다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "다시 웃기 시작하자, 불안도 잠깐 박자를 놓쳤다."
- Seasonal air: warm summer route, bright cafe light, easy routine
- Energy level: mid, sweet with a faint shadow

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: warm summer groove, light bass, brushed percussion, soft electric piano
- Tempo feel: comfortable loop
- Vocal distance: relaxed and close
- Arrangement: sweeter than E045 but keep one low shadow note

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: repeated date route, summer cup, easy laugh, missed pause
- Point of view: relationship happiness with overconfidence seed
- Words to avoid: forever, perfect rhythm, never wrong, destiny
- Do not overstate: Happiness returns, but the flaw remains.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No glossy summer hit imitation. Keep it original and slightly shaded.

## Delivery

The song should be sweet enough to make the coming crack matter.

```text
Warm Korean summer campus relationship theme, light bass, brushed percussion, soft electric piano, gentle guitar color, relaxed close vocal. Mood: happiness returning after tension, repeated date route, summer cup, easy laugh, but with one faint shadow under the warmth. Avoid forever, perfect rhythm, never wrong, destiny, and glossy summer-hit imitation.
```
