# E119 Song Brief - 침묵의 완성

## Identity

- Episode: E119
- Song function: Ending reflection
- Emotional thesis: 듣지 못해도 흔들리지 않는 상태가 완성이다

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "아무것도 들리지 않았지만, 이번에는 이상하게도 틀리지 않았다고 생각했다"
- Seasonal air: 거리, 캠퍼스 계단, 비어 있는 공기
- Energy level: low, clear, still

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: sparse piano, quiet room air, restrained low sustain, air-first ending
- Tempo feel: stable silence
- Vocal distance: medium-close, reflective
- Arrangement: keep the ending still rather than swelling

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: empty air, a hand on earphones, questions that land softly
- Point of view: Seojun standing inside silence without panic
- Words to avoid: miracle, recovery, tragedy climax
- Do not overstate: Silence is completion, not emptiness.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No hearing-return cue, no miracle recovery, no tragedy climb.

## Delivery

The song should feel like air that no longer needs to prove itself.

```text
Korean ending reflection track, sparse piano, quiet room air, restrained low sustain, air-first ending, medium-close reflective vocal. Mood: a street, campus steps, and a space so empty that it no longer feels dangerous. Avoid miracle language, recovery framing, tragedy climax, and famous song imitation.
```
