## Cut List

1. Phone screen: a short message is sent.
2. Waiting thumb: the sender rereads the message.
3. Cafe entrance: the other person arrives.
4. Overlapped hello: both start speaking at once.
5. Seated gap: the table distance stays visible.
6. Shared laugh: brief, restrained, and not loud enough to resolve anything.
7. Direct face look: the face must be read rather than guessed.
8. Low-light pause: the room keeps the conversation contained.
9. Small turn: neither person reaches for a shortcut.
10. Lingering exit air: the scene holds after the conversation shifts.
