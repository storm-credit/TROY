# E078 Song Brief - 결별

## Identity

- Episode: E078
- Song function: Relationship rupture theme
- Emotional thesis: 끝난 것은 사랑이 아니라, 서로를 계속 다치게 하던 방식이었다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "사랑은 남았지만, 이 방식으로는 더 갈 수 없었다."
- Seasonal air: Cafe Dal table, one cup left, chair shift, wet street
- Energy level: mid, most painful but most restrained

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: acceptance wound, withheld refrain, unresolved final chord, misaligned motif fragments
- Tempo feel: quiet decision
- Vocal distance: close, steady, wounded
- Arrangement: align fragments briefly, then let them fail to land together

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: one sentence, chair sound, cup left behind, opposite steps
- Point of view: clear breakup without hatred
- Words to avoid: goodbye forever, never loved, final destiny, dramatic collapse
- Do not overstate: The breakup is clear, but the love was real.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No oversung breakup climax, no final farewell cadence, no famous ballad imitation.

## Delivery

The song should hurt because it is quiet enough to be honest.

```text
Restrained Korean relationship-rupture theme, acceptance wound, withheld refrain, unresolved final chord, misaligned motif fragments, close steady wounded vocal. Mood: Cafe Dal table, one cup left, chair shift after a quiet sentence, opposite steps into a wet street, clear breakup without hatred. Avoid goodbye-forever lyrics, never-loved framing, dramatic collapse, oversung climax, final farewell cadence, and famous song imitation.
```
