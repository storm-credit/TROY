# E001 Visual Cut List

## episode

- id: E001
- title anchor: 무료한 일상
- song function: Seojun inner monologue
- MV type: mood-driven character intro
- recommended still count: 8

## visual thesis

세상은 봄인데, 서준에게만 아직 공기가 회색이고 잠겨 있다.

## master look prompt

```text
Cinematic 16:9 still for episode 1 of a Korean campus romance.
A 22-year-old Korean male university student with a quiet, guarded face, charcoal hoodie, wired earphones, understated campus fashion.
Late spring campus atmosphere, thin sunlight, long shadows, muted gray-blue palette, emotional isolation, realistic Korean university environment.
Keep the same face design, same hoodie silhouette, same wired earphones, same subdued color grammar across all episode 1 shots.
No text, no logo, no watermark.
```

## tool plan

- style exploration: Midjourney
- production stills: Nano Banana
- hero refinement if needed: Imagen

## cut list

### cut 1. 캠퍼스 중앙 길 와이드

- purpose: 봄 캠퍼스와 서준의 고립을 동시에 소개
- shot: wide
- motion intent: slow push-in

prompt:
```text
Wide cinematic view of a Korean university campus in late spring, students moving in small groups under light blossom trees, one isolated male student in a charcoal hoodie and wired earphones walking alone, thin sunlight, muted gray-blue mood, emotional distance, 16:9
```

### cut 2. 이어폰 클로즈업

- purpose: 이어폰을 차단 장치로 각인
- shot: insert close-up
- motion intent: static with subtle hand movement

prompt:
```text
Close-up of a young Korean male student's hand adjusting wired earphones, charcoal hoodie sleeve, soft spring wind, shallow depth of field, restrained gray-blue palette, tactile and quiet, 16:9
```

### cut 3. 강의실 맨 뒷자리

- purpose: 사람들 속에서도 떨어져 있는 서준
- shot: medium-wide from aisle
- motion intent: gentle lateral drift

prompt:
```text
Back row classroom shot in a Korean university, many students in front, one quiet male student alone by the window in the far back corner, sunlight on desk edge, emotional detachment, cinematic realism, 16:9
```

### cut 4. 창가와 유리 반사

- purpose: 심해와 차단의 감각을 시각화
- shot: profile medium close-up
- motion intent: slow hold

prompt:
```text
Profile shot of a Korean male student by a classroom window, glass reflection layering over his face, thin spring light, distant expression, calm but closed-off mood, muted gray-blue palette, 16:9
```

### cut 5. 학생식당 구석 자리

- purpose: 이든과의 최소한의 연결
- shot: medium two-shot
- motion intent: slight handheld warmth

prompt:
```text
Korean university cafeteria corner table, quiet male student with wired earphones sitting across from a warm friendly male roommate, lunch trays, crowded room blurred in background, one corner of warmth inside emotional distance, cinematic 16:9
```

### cut 6. 커플을 피하는 순간

- purpose: 16세 상처를 직접 설명하지 않고 회피로 표현
- shot: over-shoulder
- motion intent: subtle pull-back

prompt:
```text
Over-shoulder view of a young Korean man noticing a happy couple under blossom trees and turning away instead, campus path, spring petals, emotional recoil, cool air in warm scenery, cinematic 16:9
```

### cut 7. 밤의 기숙사 방

- purpose: 외로움의 본체 제시
- shot: medium interior
- motion intent: still frame with breathing pause

prompt:
```text
Small Korean dorm room at night, only desk lamp on, a quiet male student sitting on bed in darkness, wired earphones nearby, phone light faintly glowing, loneliness and emotional fatigue, cinematic 16:9
```

### cut 8. 천장을 보는 마지막 샷

- purpose: 다음 화의 예외를 기다리게 만드는 엔딩
- shot: top/angled close-up
- motion intent: fade-out

prompt:
```text
Close cinematic night shot of a Korean male student lying awake and staring at the ceiling, dim lamp light, shadows across the wall, unresolved loneliness, late spring night air, restrained and intimate, 16:9
```

## carry-over notes

- maintain charcoal hoodie and wired earphones in all shots
- keep spring visible but emotionally muted
- avoid overly romantic blossom imagery in episode 1
- color should stay cool until episode 2
