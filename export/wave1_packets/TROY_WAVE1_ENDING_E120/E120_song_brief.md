# E120 Song Brief - 에필로그

## Identity

- Episode: E120
- Song function: Ending reflection
- Emotional thesis: 함께하지 않아도 사랑은 남는다

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "사랑은 끝났지만, 그 여름이 거짓은 아니었다고"
- Seasonal air: 한강, 이어폰 없는 걸음, 카페 달 앞 공기
- Energy level: low, clear, dignified

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: sparse piano, restrained strings, air-forward fade, controlled lift
- Tempo feel: final calm
- Vocal distance: medium-close, reflective
- Arrangement: keep the ending dignified and air-filled

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: river, quiet walking, remaining air
- Point of view: Seojun approving the truth of the past
- Words to avoid: reunion, fate, victory lap, sequel hook
- Do not overstate: This is an approval of what remains, not a return.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No reunion fantasy, no triumphal ending, no glow overload.

## Delivery

The song should feel like air settling after a long, honest season.

```text
Korean dignified ending reflection, sparse piano, restrained strings, air-forward fade, controlled lift, medium-close reflective vocal. Mood: a cold but clear Han River walk, no earphones, and the approval that the summer was never a lie. Avoid reunion framing, fate language, victory lap, sequel hook, and famous song imitation.
```
