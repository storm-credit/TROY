# E029 Song Brief - 고백

## Identity

- Episode: E029
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 들을 수 없는 대답을 기다리기로 한 순간, 고백은 시작된다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "말하고 나서야, 나는 처음으로 기다리는 사람이 되었다."
- Seasonal air: cafe window, late light, table silence
- Energy level: mid, intimate, emotionally central

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: intimate piano ballad, restrained strings, quiet room tone, soft guitar undercurrent
- Tempo feel: slow but steady
- Vocal distance: close, vulnerable, not theatrical
- Arrangement: one controlled lift in final chorus; no triumphant climax

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: folded paper, two cups, window light, waiting silence
- Point of view: relationship threshold
- Words to avoid: forever, destiny, soulmate, grand promise, all-or-nothing
- Do not overstate: This is a small brave confession, not a melodramatic finale.

## Safe Prompt Rule

Do not imitate famous confession songs. Make it original, restrained, and room-scale.

## Delivery

The song should feel like speech becoming vulnerability.

```text
Intimate Korean romance ballad for a small cafe confession, close vulnerable vocal, soft piano, restrained strings, quiet room tone, subtle guitar undercurrent. Mood: saying the truth and waiting for an answer that cannot be controlled, folded paper, two cups, window light, table silence. Avoid forever, destiny, soulmate, grand promise, and triumphant climax. Keep it restrained, human, and ethically tender.
```
