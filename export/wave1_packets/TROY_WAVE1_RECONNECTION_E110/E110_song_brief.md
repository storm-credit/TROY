# E110 Song Brief - 다시 이어지는 리듬

## Identity

- Episode: E110
- Song function: Relationship theme
- Emotional thesis: 미래를 확정하지 않아도, 같은 박자로 잠깐 걸을 수 있는 현재가 생겼다.

## Core Feeling

- Chorus emotion line: "손이 없어도 같은 박자로 걷는 밤이, 우리를 조금 덜 두렵게 했다."
- Seasonal air: 긴 복도 그림자, 같은 방향의 보행, 늦가을의 차가운 숨, 과장되지 않은 웃음
- Energy level: low, poised, quietly warm

## Sound Direction

- Sonic palette: clear piano motif fragments, soft low synth, gentle brushed rhythm, open air tail
- Tempo feel: parallel walking cadence
- Vocal distance: medium-close, steady and human
- Arrangement: allow a modest lift but stop short of arrival

## Lyrical Direction

- Dominant image family: long shadow, same-direction walk, no-touch continuity, present-tense calm
- Point of view: a relationship finding rhythm without possession
- Words to avoid: forever, sealed reunion, complete peace, happy ending
- Do not overstate: Foundation laid, nothing final yet.

## Safe Prompt Rule

No forever hook, no reunion-locked chorus, no ending-credit glow.

## Delivery

The song should feel like a reliable rhythm returning before any final promise exists.

```text
Korean late-autumn relationship track, clear piano motif fragments, soft low synth, gentle brushed rhythm, open air tail, medium-close steady vocal. Mood: long corridor shadows, same-direction walk without touch, present-tense agreement on pace and distance, modest smile that does not oversell hope. Avoid forever language, sealed-reunion framing, complete-peace glow, ending-credit climax, and famous song imitation.
```
