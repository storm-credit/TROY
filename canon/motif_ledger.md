# Motif Ledger

## 1. Rule

복선과 모티프를 구분한다.

- foreshadow: later plot meaning
- motif: repeated emotional image

## 2. Core Motifs

### Earphones

- first meaning: self-protection, distance
- middle meaning: habit of control
- ending meaning: something that can be removed

### Paper

- first meaning: visible trace, honesty of marks
- middle meaning: lyrics, notes, diary, revision
- ending meaning: what remains after voice passes

### Window / Reflected Face

- first meaning: distance within proximity
- middle meaning: misalignment and observation
- ending meaning: seeing without possession

### Stair / Corridor

- first meaning: approach and hesitation
- middle meaning: wrong timing, uneven pace
- ending meaning: same path, different exits

### Wind

- first meaning: movement before language
- middle meaning: seasonal evidence of change
- ending meaning: acceptance without grasping

### Cafe Dal Light

- first meaning: emotional refuge
- middle meaning: misread intimacy
- ending meaning: clear but not possessive warmth

## 3. Seasonal Motif Rule

계절은 장식이 아니라 정서적 시간의 증거다.

- spring: thin light, beginning air
- summer: heat, density, fast breath
- late summer/rain: spread, dampness, delayed words
- autumn: transparency, long shadows, clear distance
- late autumn: firmness, honesty, cold clarity
